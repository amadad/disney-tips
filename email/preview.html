Disney family plan

Oct 12-18 at Pop Century

Here is the full-trip structure I would follow, with the reason each major block exists.

Family

2 adults, kids 4 and 8

Budget

Balanced

Must-dos

Slinky Dog + princess meal + fireworks + low-thrill rides

Full trip plan

Day 1

Sun Oct 12

Arrival + Pop Century

Morning: Travel, check in, and do not schedule a park.

Midday: Pool, room setup, stroller check, and grocery/snack reset.

Evening: Quick dinner at the resort. Early bedtime so Monday can start clean.

Reason: Do not burn a ticket on arrival day.

Day 2

Mon Oct 13

Magic Kingdom

Morning: Buy Lightning Lane. Rope drop Fantasyland, then Peter Pan, Small World, Haunted Mansion.

Midday: Mobile order lunch before 11:15. Leave by 1:15 for Pop Century pool/rest.

Evening: Return for two priorities, snacks, and fireworks only if everyone recovered.

Reason: This is the highest-pressure day, so protect the break.

Day 3

Tue Oct 14

Hollywood Studios

Morning: Protect Slinky Dog with rope drop or Lightning Lane. Stay in Toy Story Land first.

Midday: Early lunch, then shows or low-wait rides. Leave before the heat peak.

Evening: One character meal or Fantasmic, not both unless naps went well.

Reason: Slinky Dog is the anchor; everything else flexes around it.

Day 4

Wed Oct 15

Resort morning + EPCOT

Morning: Sleep in. No rope drop. Use the Skyliner advantage after the morning crowd surge.

Midday: Enter EPCOT after rest, aim for Frozen or Remy, and keep food choices simple.

Evening: Early dinner and one nighttime ride. Skip fireworks if the kids are fading.

Reason: This is the reset day that keeps the back half usable.

Day 5

Thu Oct 16

Animal Kingdom half day

Morning: Arrive early for safari, animals, and one headliner if waits are friendly.

Midday: Leave after lunch. Do not force a full-day Animal Kingdom plan with this party.

Evening: Resort dinner, laundry, and an early night before the final Magic Kingdom day.

Reason: Skip advance Lightning Lane unless waits spike that week.

Day 6

Fri Oct 17

Magic Kingdom redo

Morning: Repeat the kids favorite land first. Use this day to catch anything missed.

Midday: Character meal if the preferred reservation is available; otherwise quick service and rest.

Evening: Fireworks backup night. If Monday worked, leave early and end on an easy win.

Reason: This is the memory-protection day, not a cram day.

Day 7

Sat Oct 18

Departure

Morning: No park. Breakfast, packing, and one resort photo before travel.

Midday: Leave buffer for transportation delays and tired kids.

Evening: Do nothing scheduled.

Reason: A clean departure beats squeezing in one more ride.

Trip-level choices

1

Skip

Park Hopper

Do not buy Park Hopper for this trip.

Because: Your resort makes EPCOT and Hollywood Studios easy, but midday park transfers add friction for a 4-year-old.

2

Lightning Lane for MK + HS

Buy for Magic Kingdom and Hollywood Studios only.

Because: Those are the two days where it protects the must-dos and keeps the afternoon break intact.

Because: A single anchor meal gives the kids the moment without turning the week into table-service logistics.

Planning notes

DFB Guide signal

Slinky Dog Dash demand moves early and stays stubborn.

Put Hollywood Studios early in the trip and protect Slinky Dog with either rope drop or Lightning Lane.

AllEars signal

Mobile order works best when you place lunch before peak hunger.

Order before the family leaves the morning ride cluster, not when everyone is already tired.

Resort transport signal

Skyliner resorts are strongest when you avoid unnecessary transfers.

Use Pop Century as an EPCOT/HS advantage, but avoid hopping just because the route exists.

Booking timeline

Now

Decide tickets without Park Hopper.

60 days out

Book one character meal and one backup time.

7 days out

Choose Lightning Lane priorities for MK and HS.

Park day

Mobile order lunch before the morning group gets hungry.

Bad-day backup

If heat or rain breaks the day, cut the lowest-priority show, rest at Pop Century, and move fireworks to the next Magic Kingdom night.
