# 2026 Disney Changes (Synthesis Page)

A chronological catalog of known 2026 Walt Disney World changes, reopenings,
closures, and dated events. The trip planner reads this page when a user's
trip dates fall in a transition period to answer "what's different about
Disney during my trip."

This is a **synthesis page** — it pulls from multiple sources and should
grow with every ingest that adds a time-bounded claim. Maintain a new
page per calendar year.

## 2026 calendar

### Pre-2026 events that still affect 2026 trips

- **2025-07-07**: Tom Sawyer Island and Liberty Square Riverboat (Magic
  Kingdom) closed permanently to make way for Piston Peak National Park
  and Disney Villains Land. Rivers of America drained. Back-of-park
  construction will be visible throughout 2026.
  *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

- **Sometime before 2026-04-08**: "It's Tough to Be a Bug" closed at
  Animal Kingdom and was replaced by a new
  [Zootopia](zootopia-better-zoogether.md) 3D/4D theater show in the
  same Tree of Life theater. Exact closure and reopening dates not
  captured by ingested sources — open question for future ingests.
  *(Per ProvostParkPass 2026-04, firming AllEars 2026-04 which did
  not cover this change.)*


### January 2026

- **Jan 7–11**: **Walt Disney World Marathon Weekend** (RunDisney). Races
  cut through Magic Kingdom; expect elevated crowds and early park
  restrictions. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

- **Jan 12 onward**: **Disney After Hours at Magic Kingdom** ticketed event
  nights begin. Runs through 2026-07-27 on select nights. Includes
  Disney Enchantment exclusive fireworks and 20+ attractions with reduced
  waits, plus included snacks. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

- **Jan 16 – Feb 23**: **EPCOT International Festival of the Arts**.
  Opening weekend is one of the most crowded ways to do the festival;
  middle dates are calmer. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-01.)*

- **Jan 12 – Mar 13**: **Lime Garage at Disney Springs closes** for
  structural repairs. Orange and Grapefruit garages remain available.
  Partial reopening planned for **Feb 11–17** to absorb holiday-week
  traffic. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

### February – March 2026

- **2026-02-02**: **DinoLand U.S.A.'s final phase closes** and the area
  shifts fully into construction mode for
  [Tropical Americas](tropical-americas.md). This confirms that the
  former DinoLand footprint is effectively gone for all 2026 Animal
  Kingdom trips. Tropical Americas is not expected to open until later
  2027. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-03.)*

- **2026-02-23**: **Wildlife Express train and broader Rafiki's Planet
  Watch access close** to prepare the Conservation Station area for
  [Bluey's World](bluey-world.md). DFBGuide 2026-03 later confirms the
  wider area is shut and says the **Affection Section** is expected to
  return in some form, but the future of Rafiki's Planet Watch overall
  remains unclear. *(Per AllEars 2026-04, expanded by DFBGuide 2026-03.)*

- **February 2026 (exact date not captured)**: **[Frozen Ever After](frozen-ever-after.md)**
  was expected to reopen later in the month after its animatronic
  face-upgrade closure. Exact reopen date still not captured by the
  ingested sources.
  *(Per DFBGuide 2026-02.)*

- **Feb 26 – Mar 2**: **Disney Princess Half Marathon Weekend** (RunDisney).
  *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

### March 2026

- **Mar 1**: **[Rock 'n' Roller Coaster](rock-n-roller-coaster.md)**
  reaches the final scheduled day of the Aerosmith version before the
  Muppets overlay closure / transition period. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-01.)*

- **Mar 4 – Jun 1**: **EPCOT International Flower & Garden Festival**.
  Spring break overlaps make some of this run materially busier than the
  prettiest marketing photos imply. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-01.)*

- **Mar 19**: **Garden View Tea Room** reopens at
  [Grand Floridian](grand-floridian-resort.md) with a refreshed Alice in
  Wonderland-themed tea experience. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-03.)*

### April 2026

- **Early April**: **[Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin](buzz-lightyear-space-ranger-spin.md)
  is officially reopened** at Magic Kingdom, with rebuilt gameplay
  (handheld blasters, dynamic scoring, tunnel scene as the high-scoring
  zone, target-registration timed to blaster audio cue). Reopening-week
  waits running 45–60 minutes.
  *(Per TheTimTracker 2026-04-09, firming AllEars 2026-04 and
  DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

- **Apr 16–20**: **RunDisney Springtime Surprise Weekend**. Road closures
  and elevated crowds likely during the race window.
  *(Per DFBGuide 2026-02, refining the earlier DFBGuide 2025-12 range.)*

- **Apr 29** *(Disneyland only, not WDW)*: **Galaxy's Edge timeline
  expansion begins at Disneyland** — Darth Vader, Luke Skywalker, Han
  Solo, and Leia returning to the land, plus John Williams music playing
  throughout Galaxy's Edge. Walt Disney World's Galaxy's Edge has **not**
  received an equivalent announcement and remains strictly in the
  sequel-trilogy timeline. *(Per MickeyViews 2026-04.)*

### May 2026

- **May 3**: **[Big Thunder Mountain Railroad](big-thunder-mountain-railroad.md)
  officially reopens** at Magic Kingdom after its 2025 refurbishment.
  Height requirement lowered from 40" to 38". Trains were actively
  testing during the two days before announcement. Expect rope-drop
  demand and Lightning Lane competition during the opening window.
  *(Per TheTimTracker 2026-04-09 — first firm date after DFBGuide
  2025-12's "sometime in 2026, no date" and AllEars 2026-04's "about
  to reopen.")*

- **Memorial Day period**: **Soarin' Across America** temporarily replaces
  Soarin' Around the World at EPCOT as part of the America's 250th
  anniversary push. A brief closure window to swap the film is possible,
  but exact dates were not captured.
  *(Per DFBGuide 2026-02.)*

- **May 2026 through sometime in August 2026**:
  **Yachtsman Steakhouse** at
  [Yacht Club](yacht-club-resort.md) closes for refurbishment. Adjacent
  Crew's Cup Lounge was also temporarily closed at source time but was
  expected to reopen in May. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-04.)*

- **May 22**: **[Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run](millennium-falcon-smugglers-run.md)**
  at Hollywood Studios gets a content update — new name
  ("A New Mission"), Mandalorian and Grogu storyline, and
  choose-your-destination gameplay (Bespin, second Death Star wreckage,
  Endor, Coruscant). Attraction remains open through the update.
  *(Per MickeyViews 2026-04.)*

### Summer 2026 (exact date TBD)

- **[Rock 'n' Roller Coaster](rock-n-roller-coaster.md)** reopens at
  Hollywood Studios with a **Muppets overlay** replacing the Aerosmith
  theme. The Electric Mayhem (Animal's band) is the new featured act.
  Ride mechanics unchanged; vehicles repainted; exterior guitar
  sculpture stays with new signage. MickeyViews 2026-04 reports the
  target as "early summer" but acknowledges no firm date. *(Per
  MickeyViews 2026-04.)*

### May – September 2026

- **May 26 – Sept 8**: **[Cool Kids Summer](cool-kids-summer.md)** — expanded
  character presence across all four parks, water parks, and Disney-owned
  resorts. Goofy Core returns at EPCOT's Communicore Hall. Resort hotel
  character meets also expand during this window. *(Per AllEars 2026-04,
  expanded by DFBGuide 2026-03.)*

- **May 26 – Sept 8**: **[Resort perk: free water park on check-in day](resort-perk-free-water-park.md)**
  is available to Disney hotel guests during this event window in 2026.
  Both **Typhoon Lagoon** and **Blizzard Beach** are expected to be open
  during the period. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-03; AllEars 2026-04 covers how
  to use the perk.)*

- **2026-05-26**: **[Bluey's World](bluey-world.md)** opens at Conservation
  Station / Rafiki's Planet Watch in Animal Kingdom. Permanent walkthrough
  attraction with Bluey + Bingo characters, games, and the adjacent
  Jumping Junction Australian wildlife area. *(Per AllEars 2026-04.)*

- **Through mid-September 2026**:
  **[Saratoga Springs](saratoga-springs-resort.md) Treehouse Villas**
  remain under renovation. Availability is reduced and some stays may
  encounter daytime construction nearby. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-04.)*

### July 2026

- **Jul 3–5**: **Disney's Celebrate America** Fourth of July fireworks at
  Magic Kingdom (replaces Happily Ever After on those nights). *(Per
  DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

- **Jul 27**: Final night of Disney After Hours at Magic Kingdom for the
  winter-spring season. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

### Late October 2026

- **Oct 22–25**: **Wine & Dine Half Marathon Weekend** (RunDisney).
  Expect the usual road-closure and crowd-pressure effects around the race
  window. *(Per DFBGuide 2026-01, firming DFBGuide 2025-12's no-date
  placeholder.)*

### December 2026

- **Dec 30–31**: **Fantasy in the Sky** New Year's fireworks at Magic
  Kingdom (7:30pm and 11:50pm on both nights). 2025 schedule; 2026
  repeat expected but not yet confirmed. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

### August – October 2026

- **Mickey's Not-So-Scary Halloween Party** — seasonal after-hours
  ticketed event at Magic Kingdom, typically running on select nights
  from August through October. 2026 specific dates TBD. Think Disney
  After Hours but longer, with more holiday entertainment, seasonal
  character overlays, exclusive characters, and free candy trails on
  event nights. Magic Kingdom closes early to general admission guests
  (~6 PM) on party nights. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

### Later 2026 (exact date TBD)

- **[Walt Disney Studios lot](walt-disney-studios-lot.md)** opens at
  Hollywood Studios, replacing the former Animation Courtyard. Includes
  the updated Magic of Disney Animation attraction (with the
  controversial Olaf-animatronic Animation Academy), new Disney Junior
  Mickey Mouse Clubhouse Live interactive show, meet-and-greets with
  Mulan/Rapunzel/Chip and Dale/Donald/Daisy/Goofy/Stitch, and a new
  planter/park courtyard area for families. Facade modeled on Disney
  Feature Animation in California. *(Per MickeyViews 2026-04.)*

- **Level 99 opens at Disney Springs** sometime in 2026. This will be the
  venue's first Florida location and adds a substantial challenge-room /
  social-entertainment option to the district. Exact opening date not yet
  captured. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

### November – December 2026

- **Mickey's Very Merry Christmas Party** — seasonal after-hours
  ticketed event at Magic Kingdom, typically running on select nights
  from November through December. 2026 specific dates TBD. Same
  after-hours format as the Halloween Party, with holiday entertainment,
  hot cocoa and cookies on event nights, and seasonal character
  overlays. Park closes early to general admission (~6 PM) on party
  nights. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

## Changes with no firm date yet

- **Cinderella Castle repaint** — **actively in progress as of
  April 2026**, working around the castle systematically. TheTimTracker
  2026-04-09 observed: one side of the castle is already repainted to
  the gray color scheme (which TheTimTracker describes as "back to what
  they were before the 50th celebration"), while the Fantasyland-side
  facade is still in its pink 50th-anniversary colors. Construction
  scrims not currently blocking views.
  *(Per TheTimTracker 2026-04-09 — supersedes DFBGuide 2025-12's
  "announced but not scheduled" and implies the target colors are
  pre-50th rather than DFBGuide's "original blue-and-white" claim —
  discrepancy noted, needs clarification.)*

- **Carousel of Progress** — new opening scene featuring a Walt Disney
  animatronic similar to the one at Disneyland. Announced but not
  scheduled. May cause closures when installed. *(Per DFBGuide 2025-12.)*

- **Piston Peak National Park** and **Disney Villains Land** — two
  entirely new lands on the former back-of-Magic-Kingdom footprint. Both
  "years down the road" per DFBGuide; not on any 2026 trip itinerary.

- **[Monstropolis](monstropolis.md)** — Monsters Inc.-themed land at
  Hollywood Studios replacing the former Muppets Courtyard. Includes
  the Monsters Inc. Door Factory Coaster (e-ticket suspended coaster),
  Glob Theater (Mike Wazowski attraction replacing Muppet Vision 3D),
  and a sushi restaurant + open courtyard dining. MickeyViews 2026-04
  speculates 2027 or 2028 opening; no firm date. Not a 2026 trip
  consideration. *(Per MickeyViews 2026-04.)*
