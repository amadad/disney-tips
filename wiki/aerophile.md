# Aerophile

Disney Springs tethered-balloon attraction offering short flights high above
the district. One of the most visually distinctive things to do outside the
parks, but also one of the most weather-dependent.

## What it is

Aerophile is an **8-minute** tethered balloon flight that can carry up to
**29 guests** and rise to around **400 feet**. On a clear day, DFBGuide says
views can stretch roughly **10 miles**.

## Timing and weather

Aerophile is a **sunny-day play**, not a reliable all-conditions activity.
Wind and weather determine whether it operates.

DFBGuide also notes a secondary appeal: if the timing lines up right, a later
flight may give you distant views of Disney fireworks.

## Worth it for

- guests who want a non-ride Disney Springs headline activity
- photography-minded visitors
- non-park days with clear weather

## Not worth it for

- rainy or windy itineraries
- guests who want something guaranteed to operate on schedule
