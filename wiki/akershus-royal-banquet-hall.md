# Akershus Royal Banquet Hall

Norway pavilion princess character meal in EPCOT.
DFBGuide's strongest value case for a princess dining experience at Walt
Disney World — **if you book breakfast**.

## Why breakfast is the move

Akershus is one of the clearest Disney Dining Plan optimization plays in
EPCOT:

- **Breakfast** costs **one** table-service credit
- **Lunch and dinner** cost **two** table-service credits

DFBGuide's judgment is that breakfast is the sweet spot because you still
get the princess character experience without burning the heavier,
pricier lunch/dinner cost structure.

## What you're paying for

This is a princess meal first. DFBGuide positions it as one of the best
ways to get that experience without paying the full inside-the-castle
premium of [Cinderella's Royal Table](cinderellas-royal-table.md).

## Worth it for

- families prioritizing princess dining
- EPCOT days that need one major sit-down anchor
- Disney Dining Plan users trying to stretch table-service value
