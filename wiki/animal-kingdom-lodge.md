# Animal Kingdom Lodge

Deluxe Disney resort built around **real savannas with live animals**.
One of the strongest hotels on property for people who want the resort
itself to feel like a major part of the trip.

## Why it stands out

Animal Kingdom Lodge is not just theming. It has:

- **four separate savannas**
- **200+ animals** across those savannas
- strong resort-day activities
- some of Disney World's most distinctive hotel food

DFBGuide's blunt framing is that this is one of Disney World's true
**food hotels**.

## Signature experience

The resort's premium extra is the **Starlight Safari**:

- about **1 hour**
- guided after-dark safari tour
- guests use **night-vision goggles**
- possible animals include zebra, giraffe, gazelle, antelope,
  flamingos, and more

## Layout

Animal Kingdom Lodge is split into two sections:

- **Jambo House** — main building, classic lodge identity, popular
  savanna-view rooms
- **Kidani Village** — Disney Vacation Club side with studios and larger
  villa layouts

Kidani is the side for guests who need bigger room formats like one-,
two-, or three-bedroom villas.

## 2026 refurbishment watchout

This resort is not static in 2026.
DFBGuide 2026-03 reports:

- **Kidani Village** refurbishment runs through **May 2026**
- then **Jambo House** enters its own refurbishment window from
  **May 2026 through January 2027**

This does not erase the resort's value, but it does matter if you are
booking specifically for a quieter resort-heavy stay.

## Dining signal

DFBGuide strongly highlights the food here, especially:

- the broad African-and-American dining mix
- **Boma breakfast** with Simba waffles, carved ham, and endless POG
  juice

## Best for

- guests who want a **resort day** that still feels special
- food-first travelers
- animal-loving families
- travelers willing to trade monorail / Skyliner convenience for hotel
  atmosphere

## Watch outs

- this is not a transportation-convenience hotel
- the splurge only makes sense if you care about the resort itself,
  not just the room
