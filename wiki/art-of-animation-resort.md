# Art of Animation Resort

Disney's most aggressively themed **value resort**.
Pricier than the other value hotels, but built to win on family suites,
pool theming, and Skyliner access.

## Why people pay more for it

DFBGuide's case for Art of Animation comes down to three things:

1. **room theming**
2. **pool theming**
3. **Skyliner access**

## Rooms

Themed around major Disney / Pixar films:

- **Little Mermaid** — standard rooms
- **Cars** — family suites
- **The Lion King** — family suites
- **Finding Nemo** — family suites

This makes Art of Animation one of the strongest hotels for families who
want the room itself to feel like part of the vacation.

AllEars 2026-03 adds the main budget warning: the family suites often
run about **$500–$700+ per night**. At that point, Art of Animation is
not the cheap Disney lane anymore. It is a themed-space splurge.

## Pools

DFBGuide especially highlights the pool theming:

- **Big Blue Pool** (Finding Nemo) — biggest feature pool on Disney
  property per the source
- **Flip Flop / Little Mermaid pool**
- **Cozy Cone pool** (Cars) — with first-come, first-served cone-shaped
  cabanas

## Transportation

Art of Animation is on the **Skyliner**, giving direct-style access to:

- EPCOT
- Hollywood Studios
- other Skyliner resorts

## Best for

- families who want heavily Disney-themed suites
- guests who care a lot about pool theming
- travelers who want Skyliner access but prefer stronger Disney theming
  than [pop-century-resort.md](pop-century-resort.md)

## Not best for

- budget travelers who just want the cheapest useful room
- adults-only trips where the suite theming is not worth the higher
  value-resort price
