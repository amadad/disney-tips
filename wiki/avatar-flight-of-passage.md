# Avatar Flight of Passage

Animal Kingdom's highest-demand ride and the park's most important
Lightning Lane splurge.
A motion-simulator attraction in [Pandora](pandora-world-of-avatar.md)
where guests "fly" on a banshee.

## Why it matters

Flight of Passage is the ride that distorts Animal Kingdom morning
strategy.
If you are using **Early Theme Park Entry**, this is where a huge share
of on-site guests will go first.

## Lightning Lane

DFBGuide 2026-04 treats Flight of Passage as the **single most useful
Lightning Lane purchase in Animal Kingdom**.

The reasoning is simple:

- it is the park's hardest standby line
- many Early Entry guests will rope-drop it
- buying the Single Pass here lets you use your early-entry window for
  other rides instead

## Smarter use of Early Entry

If you already hold a Flight of Passage Single Pass, DFBGuide's logic is
to use your early-entry time on easier wins instead, such as:

- Na'vi River Journey
- Expedition Everest
- Kilimanjaro Safaris

That is often a better use of the 30-minute head start than joining the
big Flight of Passage rush.

## Best for

- Animal Kingdom days where you want one paid line-skip that actually
  changes the feel of the morning
- groups who do not want to bet their whole opening move on one ride
- guests who care about headliners more than an Animal Kingdom-wide
  Multi Pass purchase
