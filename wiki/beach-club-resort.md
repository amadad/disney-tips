# Beach Club Resort

Deluxe EPCOT-area resort with a light coastal vibe and one of the best
hotel locations in Walt Disney World.

## Why it stands out

Beach Club's core advantage is **location**.
DFBGuide frames it as the hotel **closest to EPCOT's International
Gateway**, which means you are basically on top of the back entrance.
Hollywood Studios is also walkable, though not nearly as close.

## Signature strengths

- quick access to EPCOT's back entrance
- walk option to Hollywood Studios
- **Beaches & Cream** 1950s-style restaurant / ice cream stop
- access to **Stormalong Bay**, shared with
  [yacht-club-resort.md](yacht-club-resort.md)

## Stormalong Bay

This is not just a pool. DFBGuide frames it as a mini water park with:

- sand-bottom pool
- lazy river
- three whirlpool spas
- tanning deck
- long shipwreck-themed water slide

## 2026–2027 maintenance note

DFBGuide 2026-03 says both Beach Club and
[Yacht Club](yacht-club-resort.md) are under routine exterior
maintenance through **2027**, but **Stormalong Bay remains open**.

## Rooms

Beach Club offers:

- standard rooms
- club level rooms
- suites
- Disney Vacation Club villas with kitchens and laundry

## Best for

- EPCOT-first trips
- adults or families who want deluxe location more than character theming
- travelers who want the strongest pool complex in this resort cluster
