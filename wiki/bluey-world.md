# Bluey's World

Upcoming permanent attraction at Disney's Animal Kingdom. **Opens 2026-05-26**
at Conservation Station / Rafiki's Planet Watch. Based on the Australian
children's show *Bluey*, one of the most-streamed television properties in
the world as of early 2026.

## What it is

A walkthrough experience (not a ride, not a pure meet-and-greet). Includes:

- Character interactions with Bluey and Bingo
- Games and activity-based experiences for guests of all ages
- Australian wildlife exhibits (adjacent area branded **Jumping Junction**)

Permanent installation, not a limited-time offering. Disney is reworking
the broader Conservation Station / Rafiki's Planet Watch area around it.

AllEars 2026-04 gives the cleaner **Bluey's World** title. DFBGuide
2026-03 transcribes the name closer to **Bluey's Wild World**; treat that
as transcript noise for now, not a firm renaming.

## Why Disney is building it

Per AllEars 2026-04, this is part of a broader play to solve Animal Kingdom's
current "not a full day park" problem (see
[animal-kingdom.md](animal-kingdom.md)). AK has only 5 operating rides right
now and guests report running out of things to do by early afternoon.
Bluey's World is designed to hold people in the park for a meaningful
additional stretch of time — the creator's framing is "adding a reason to
spend time there, not adding another ride."

Bluey has crossover audience reach — not just preschoolers but adults who
watch it independently. Previous touring "Bluey's Big Play" stage show sold
out with ticket prices $35–$100+, which Disney clearly read as demand
signal.

## Construction timeline

- **2026-02-23**: Wildlife Express train and broader Rafiki's Planet
  Watch access shut down for the Bluey-area build-out.
- **2026-05-26**: Bluey's World opens to guests.
- DFBGuide 2026-03 says the **Affection Section** is expected to return
  in some capacity, but the wider Rafiki's Planet Watch future remains
  unclear.
- Fate of the Wildlife Express train after opening is not confirmed.

## Trip planning relevance

- **Trips before 2026-05-26**: Bluey's World is not yet accessible. The
  Wildlife Express train is closed, so the Conservation Station area is
  effectively off the map for guests — do not plan to visit it. This
  also means Animal Kingdom's "half-day park" framing still applies.
- **Trips on or after 2026-05-26**: Bluey's World becomes a planning
  consideration, especially for parties with young kids or Bluey fans.
  Expect high demand during the opening window.
