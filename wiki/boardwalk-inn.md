# BoardWalk Inn

Deluxe EPCOT-area resort with a turn-of-the-century Atlantic boardwalk
identity. Best understood as a hotel with **two personalities**: cozy and
nostalgic by day, more nightlife-oriented after dark.

## Why it stands out

Like [beach-club-resort.md](beach-club-resort.md) and
[yacht-club-resort.md](yacht-club-resort.md), BoardWalk Inn is within
walking distance of EPCOT's International Gateway.

What makes it different is the vibe:

- more Atlantic City / Coney Island than beach-club softness
- stronger day-to-night mood shift
- more obvious evening bar / nightlife identity

## Day vs. night

### By day

DFBGuide calls out:

- tandem-bike rentals
- Cake Bake Shop tea / cakes
- Blue Ribbon Corn Dogs
- occasional streetmosphere acts

### By night

The strip turns more adult and nightlife-heavy, with:

- Bellevue Lounge
- AbracadaBar
- Flying Fish
- Atlantic Dance Hall on weekends for 21+

## Current and upcoming watchouts

DFBGuide 2026-03 adds several caution flags here:

- **Jellyrolls** closed in 2025 after 30 years
- **Big River Grill** remains closed
- Disney is already warning guests about substantial **2027**
  maintenance, including the **Luna Park pool**, splash / play area, and
  **Leaping Horse Libations** closing from **2027-01-11 through
  2027-04**
- the **Skyliner** is also expected to be closed from
  **2027-01-24 through 2027-01-30**, which matters for BoardWalk-area
  hotel transportation to Hollywood Studios

This is not a 2026 stay-killer, but it is a real watchout for 2027
trip planning.

## Rooms

BoardWalk Inn offers:

- standard rooms
- club level rooms
- suites
- Disney Vacation Club villas
- standout **garden rooms** — two-story, private-feeling rooms behind
  white picket fences, framed as especially romantic

## Best for

- adults-first trips
- EPCOT evenings and bar / lounge hopping
- guests who want one of the strongest hotel-after-dark atmospheres on
  property
