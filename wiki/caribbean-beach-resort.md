# Caribbean Beach Resort

Large moderate resort with colorful Caribbean-island theming and one of
the most important moderate-tier advantages on property:
**Skyliner access**.

## The tradeoff

DFBGuide presents Caribbean Beach as a true compromise resort.
Its strengths are real, but so are its annoyances.

### Strengths

- Skyliner access
- improved food reputation
- strong feature-pool theming
- lots of quiet pools

### Weaknesses

- very **sprawling** layout
- no elevators to many upper-floor rooms
- room location can significantly change the stay

## Layout

Five village areas:

- Trinidad
- Barbados
- Martinique
- Aruba
- Jamaica

Most rooms are standard layouts, but some have a **fifth-sleeper** wall
bed. DFBGuide also notes that the Trinidad section may be the place to
request if you want one of the **Little Mermaid-themed** fifth-sleeper
rooms.

## Pool / resort signal

Main feature pool is built around a **Spanish fortress** theme.
DFBGuide also highlights the resort's **six leisure pools**, which helps
this hotel function better on non-park time than many moderates.

## Practical booking tip

Because the resort is so spread out, DFBGuide explicitly recommends
considering a **preferred room** so you are closer to the main amenities.

## Best for

- moderate-budget guests who want Skyliner access
- families okay with a large resort footprint
- guests who want a stronger resort feel than value hotels without going
  full deluxe
