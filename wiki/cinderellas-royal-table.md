# Cinderella's Royal Table

Signature table-service dining **inside Cinderella Castle** at Magic
Kingdom. The most atmospheric dining experience at Walt Disney World
and the single hardest Magic Kingdom reservation to secure.

## What you're paying for

DFBGuide's framing: this is Disney magic at its peak, and you are
paying primarily for the **atmosphere and the character experience**,
not the cuisine.

The dining room is inside the castle itself — scaling stone walls,
tapestries, art, stained-glass windows. Before being seated, guests
receive a photo op with **Cinderella**. Throughout the meal,
additional princesses rotate through the dining room for
greetings and photos. Regulars include **Rapunzel, Aurora, and
Jasmine**, with Merida, Ariel, Belle, and other fan favorites
appearing at various times.

## Prices and format

Prefix menu for all meals:

- **Breakfast**: $76 per adult (cheapest option, same castle
  experience and princesses)
- **Lunch**: $89 per adult
- **Dinner**: $89 per adult

DFBGuide's framing: "This could easily be the most expensive meal you
eat all vacation. Start saving if this is a priority."

## Reservations

Advance dining reservations open **60 days before your trip**. If
princess dining is a priority for your party, Cinderella's Royal
Table is the **one reservation you need to lock in the moment the
window opens**. It is consistently the hardest ADR to secure at
Magic Kingdom.

DFBGuide 2026-01 again places it among the **hardest ADRs anywhere on
property**, not just inside Magic Kingdom. For the broader booking
mechanics, see [advanced-dining-reservations.md](advanced-dining-reservations.md).

## Worth it for

- Parties with children who want the princess experience at peak
  intensity
- Disney enthusiasts who want the inside-the-castle experience at
  least once
- Milestone trip celebrations

**Not worth it for**: parties prioritizing cuisine quality, parties
already doing Crystal Palace or another character meal, budget-
sensitive trips. DFBGuide recommends saving signature-dining budget
for **[Disney Springs](disney-springs.md) restaurants**, hotel
restaurants (California Grill at the Contemporary, 'Ohana), or
**Hollywood Brown Derby** in Hollywood Studios, where the food quality
is substantially better.
