# Contemporary Resort

One of Disney World's original deluxe hotels and still one of the most
practical splurges for Magic Kingdom trips.

## Why it stands out

Contemporary wins on **location + transportation + dining**.
DFBGuide's core case:

- walk to Magic Kingdom
- Monorail through the main A-frame
- easy EPCOT access after a Transportation and Ticket Center transfer
- one of the strongest resort dining lineups on property

## Dining signal

Key venues called out by DFBGuide:

- Contempo Cafe
- Chef Mickey's
- Steakhouse 71
- California Grill
- multiple lounge / bar options, including California Grill's bar

This is one of the clearest hotels where you can justify the resort even
if you are only going there to eat.

## Room layouts

Three main room buckets matter here:

- **main tower / A-frame rooms**
- **Garden Wing rooms** — cheaper, near the tower but not inside it
- **Bay Lake Tower** — Disney Vacation Club tower with studios and
  villas

The current room design uses **Incredibles** theming, which DFBGuide
notes may read as kid-friendlier but less sophisticated than the older
room design.

## Construction caveat

**Bay Lake Tower** is under a long renovation expected to continue
through **late 2027**, so some stays there may include visible or audible
construction during the day.

DFBGuide 2026-03 adds a more immediate 2026 detail:

- the **Bay Cove pool**, water-play area, and whirlpool spa at Bay Lake
  Tower were closed until **sometime in May 2026**
- guests can use the Contemporary's main pool area instead during that
  window

## Best for

- Magic Kingdom-first trips
- travelers who value transportation simplicity
- guests who want strong dining without leaving the resort area
