# Drawn to Life

Cirque du Soleil theatrical production at Disney Springs that blends live
acrobatic performance with Disney-animation storytelling. One of the strongest
**rainy-day and non-park-day anchors** anywhere at Walt Disney World.

## Why it matters

Drawn to Life gives Disney Springs something many guests do not expect from it:

an actual headline scheduled entertainment experience that can carry a whole
chunk of the day by itself.

DFBGuide treats it as one of the clearest Disney Springs answers to bad
weather or park fatigue.

## Upgrades and packages

Two add-on structures are specifically called out:

- **Animated Evening** — dining package tied to select Disney Springs
  restaurants plus the show
- **Page to Stage Signature Experience** — higher-end VIP package with show
  ticket, souvenirs, snacks, and backstage-tour component

## Worth it for

- rainy-day Disney plans
- adults-only evenings
- non-park-day scheduling
- families who want something big without standing in attraction lines
