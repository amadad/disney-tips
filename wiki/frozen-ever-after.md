# Frozen Ever After

Norway pavilion boat ride in World Showcase based on *Frozen*.
One of EPCOT's three core **Tier 1 Lightning Lane Multi Pass** attractions
and one of the park's most important family-demand rides.

## Early-2026 closure

At the time of DFBGuide's February 2026 EPCOT guide, Frozen Ever After
was temporarily **closed for an animatronic face upgrade**. Disney was
replacing the older projected-face figures with newer animatronic
technology, with the park expecting a **February 2026 reopening**.

Exact reopening date is **not captured by the ingested sources yet**.

## Trip-planning impact

Even when operational, Frozen Ever After is one of the rides that makes
EPCOT's World Showcase half of the park meaningfully competitive.

Key planning implications:

- it is a **Tier 1 Multi Pass** attraction
- it competes with [Remy's Ratatouille Adventure](remys-ratatouille-adventure.md)
  and [Test Track](test-track.md) for your one Tier 1 pre-selection
- if entering through **International Gateway**, Remy is the more natural
  rope-drop move because of simple geography, which makes Frozen an even
  stronger case for your Tier 1 reservation
