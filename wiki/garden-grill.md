# Garden Grill

Rotating character-meal restaurant inside The Land pavilion at EPCOT.
One of DFBGuide / AJ's clearest personal favorites in the park and one of
the strongest all-around sit-down values at EPCOT.

## Why it stands out

Garden Grill combines several things that usually live on separate Disney
restaurant shortlists:

- **character meal**
- **comfort food**
- **all-you-care-to-enjoy format**
- **rotating dining room** with views into Living with the Land

Characters called out by DFBGuide here: **Mickey, Pluto, Chip, and Dale**.

## Dining Plan value

DFBGuide's specific strategy note: if you are maximizing Disney Dining
Plan value, **lunch or dinner** is the stronger move because breakfast is
cheaper out of pocket, while lunch/dinner still cost only one
table-service credit and include the heavier harvest-feast menu.

## Why planners like it

Garden Grill is unusually good for groups that want one meal to do a lot
of work at once: sit-down break, air conditioning, characters, decent
food quantity, and a location inside one of EPCOT's most useful pavilions.
