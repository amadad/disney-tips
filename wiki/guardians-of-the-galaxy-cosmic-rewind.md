# Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind

EPCOT's marquee indoor coaster and the single hardest ride reservation in
the park. It regularly posts the **highest waits in EPCOT** and is the
park's only attraction sold as a **Single Pass** rather than as part of
Lightning Lane Multi Pass.

## Lightning Lane

Cosmic Rewind is **not** part of EPCOT's Tier 1 / Tier 2 Multi Pass
structure.

Instead, it uses **Single Pass only** — one purchase, one ride, one guest.
DFBGuide gives a rough typical price range of **$14–$23 per person**.

Her practical judgment: if you are spending Lightning Lane money in EPCOT,
**Cosmic Rewind is often the better spend than Multi Pass itself**,
because the ride's waits are so consistently high and the rest of EPCOT
has more low-wait filler than the other parks.

## Park position

Cosmic Rewind sits in **World Discovery**, much closer to EPCOT's front
entrance than to the International Gateway. That matters.

If Cosmic Rewind is your top ride priority, the **front entrance** is the
more natural starting point. International Gateway is much better aligned
with [Remy's Ratatouille Adventure](remys-ratatouille-adventure.md) and
France / UK World Showcase starts.

## Motion warning

DFBGuide jokes that the Cosmic Rewind + [Mission: Space](mission-space.md)
cluster is "**Barf Alley**" — a not-joking-enough warning for guests who
get motion sick.

Practical guidance from the current corpus:

- if your stomach is sensitive, do **not** stack Cosmic Rewind and
  Mission: Space Orange back-to-back
- DFBGuide 2026-03 also says she may skip Cosmic Rewind entirely if she
  has **not eaten yet**, because the spinning / backwards / screen-heavy
  combo can be rough on an empty stomach
- some guests may want motion-sickness meds **before** the ride instead
  of after they need them
