# Haunted Mansion

Magic Kingdom Liberty Square classic dark ride. **Opening day
attraction** — has been operating continuously since Magic Kingdom
opened in 1971. One of Disney's most iconic attractions and — with
the 2025 riverfront closures — now essentially the only remaining
headliner in Liberty Square until the Piston Peak and Villains Land
construction clears.

**Family-friendly despite the name**: DisneyInDetail 2026-04 framing:
"Despite what the name suggests, it's nothing to be scared of. It's
family-friendly spooky fun." Appropriate for most kids. The ride is
a slow-moving dark ride with gothic theming, ghostly apparitions, and
the iconic ballroom scene.

**Detail-rich on repeat rides**: DisneyInDetail (who has ridden this
attraction for 24 years) still finds new details every time. Repeat
rides reward attention — there are layers of theming packed into the
mansion interior that single-ride guests miss.

## Wait dynamics

Haunted Mansion is generally a popular wait. DFBGuide's tip: waits
**drop noticeably late in the evening near fireworks time**, as guests
start migrating toward Main Street for
[Happily Ever After](happily-ever-after.md). A late-evening ride can
be significantly shorter than a midday one.

Wait dynamics are similar to [Pirates of the Caribbean](pirates-of-the-caribbean.md)
in that both are Liberty Square / Adventureland dark-ride classics
whose queues fluctuate with park-wide events rather than staying
uniformly high all day.

## Lightning Lane

Haunted Mansion is a **Tier 2** Multi Pass attraction at Magic Kingdom
(Tier 1 is reserved for Jungle Cruise, Space Mountain, Peter Pan, and
Tiana's). This means it doesn't compete for your single Tier 1
pre-selection slot — you can stack it into your pre-selections without
cost to other priorities.

See [lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md).
