# Disney's Hollywood Studios

Walt Disney World's film-and-TV park. In 2026, Hollywood Studios is in
the middle of a **major construction push** — multiple headliners are
closed, a large new area is opening (Walt Disney Studios lot), and a
future Monsters Inc.-themed land is under construction in the former
Muppets Courtyard. This is the park most in flux during 2026.

## State in 2026

**Closed / overlaying:**

- **[Rock 'n' Roller Coaster](rock-n-roller-coaster.md)** — closed for
  a Muppets-themed overlay, reopening summer 2026 (per MickeyViews
  2026-04, aiming for early summer). Same ride, new theme.

**Opening in 2026:**

- **[Walt Disney Studios lot](walt-disney-studios-lot.md)** — the
  former Animation Courtyard, fully renamed and reworked, opening
  later in 2026 with an updated Magic of Disney Animation
  attraction, an Olaf-led Animation Academy, a Disney Junior show,
  and a new courtyard park area.

**May 22, 2026:**

- **[Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run](millennium-falcon-smugglers-run.md)**
  — gets a content update with a new mission featuring Mandalorian
  and Grogu, and choose-your-destination gameplay. The attraction
  itself is continuously open through the update.

**Not opening in 2026 (2027–2028 estimate):**

- **[Monstropolis](monstropolis.md)** — Monsters Inc.-themed land
  replacing the former Muppets Courtyard. Foundation work underway
  for an e-ticket suspended coaster; facade demolition visible on
  the former Muppet Vision 3D theater. Not on any 2026 trip
  itinerary.

## Attractions (current)

### Sunset Boulevard

- **The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror** — operating, no changes
  announced
- [Rock 'n' Roller Coaster](rock-n-roller-coaster.md) — closed for
  Muppets overlay

### Hollywood Boulevard / Echo Lake

- **Mickey and Minnie's Runaway Railway** — operating. Notably, Disney
  has been doing more spontaneous character encounters in this area,
  including Mary Poppins characters meeting guests informally outside
  the attraction (per MickeyViews 2026-04).
- **Star Tours** — operating. The nearby Jedi Training Academy stage
  has been closed since 2020 and has not reopened. MickeyViews argues
  (editorially) that it should come back, citing lost viral/marketing
  value.

### Animation Courtyard → Walt Disney Studios lot (opening later 2026)

See [walt-disney-studios-lot.md](walt-disney-studios-lot.md).

### Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge

- **[Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run](millennium-falcon-smugglers-run.md)**
  — operating, getting new mission May 22, 2026
- **[Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance](star-wars-rise-of-the-resistance.md)**
  — operating. One of the park's biggest Lightning Lane tradeoff rides:
  important, but not always the smartest Hollywood Studios-specific paid
  purchase if it means giving up broader Multi Pass coverage.

**Note on Galaxy's Edge timeline**: Walt Disney World's Galaxy's Edge
currently remains in the sequel trilogy timeline. Disneyland is
expanding its Galaxy's Edge timeline beginning April 29, 2026 (Darth
Vader, Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, and Leia returning, plus John
Williams music throughout the land). WDW has not announced any
equivalent update as of MickeyViews 2026-04.

### Toy Story Land

- **[Slinky Dog Dash](slinky-dog-dash.md)** — operating. One of the
  park's most important Tier 1 Lightning Lane priorities.
- **Toy Story Mania** — operating (referenced by creator as a
  comparison to rebuilt Buzz Lightyear gameplay, and treated by DFBGuide
  2026-04 as a strong Tier 2 Lightning Lane pre-selection)
- **Alien Swirling Saucers** — operating
- **New popcorn-themed snack stand** — opened recently between Toy
  Story Land and the Galaxy's Edge pathway. Pizza Planet spring rolls
  on the menu, plus Jack Daniels whiskey and Coke. MickeyViews notes
  the theming detail (toothpick overhangs, popcorn-container framing)
  and the thematic odd-pairing of an adult cocktail in "Andy's
  backyard." A new seating area nearby accommodates guests between
  the two lands.

## Trip planning implications

2026 Hollywood Studios is shaped by timing:

- **Trips before summer 2026**: Rock 'n' Roller Coaster is closed. The
  Walt Disney Studios lot is still under construction. Monstropolis is
  a hard-hat zone. Expect reduced attraction count.
- **Trips summer 2026+**: Rock 'n' Roller Coaster should be open with
  Muppets theme. Millennium Falcon has new mission content.
- **Trips late 2026**: Walt Disney Studios lot adds a new area worth
  visiting, especially for families with young kids.
- **Trips during Jollywood Nights season**: remember that party nights
  can cut regular-admission evenings short, with non-party guests out
  around **6:30 PM** per DFBGuide 2026-03.
- **Trips 2027+**: Monstropolis opens (tentative), adding a new e-ticket
  to the park.

Hollywood Studios does not currently risk the "not enough to do" framing
Animal Kingdom is facing — the existing roster ([Slinky Dog Dash](slinky-dog-dash.md),
[Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance](star-wars-rise-of-the-resistance.md),
Millennium Falcon, Tower of Terror, Runaway Railway, Toy
Story Mania, Star Tours) is still substantial even with Rock 'n' Roller
closed.

DFBGuide 2026-04 adds an important planning judgment: if you can only buy
**one** Lightning Lane product here, [lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md)
is often smarter than buying only Rise's Single Pass, because Hollywood
Studios has so many other high-demand rides competing for your day.

DFBGuide 2026-02 adds two useful crowd-timing tricks:

- **Galaxy's Edge** can feel calmer once the nighttime crowd shifts
  toward the front of the park for **Fantasmic**
- after Fantasmic, the **walking path to BoardWalk** can be the lowest-
  stress exit if you want to avoid the immediate post-show transport surge
