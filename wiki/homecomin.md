# Chef Art Smith's Homecomin'

Disney Springs southern restaurant known for fried chicken, comfort food,
weekend brunch, and moonshine cocktails. One of the most in-demand Disney
Springs reservations and one of DFBGuide's default "you should do this at
least once" recommendations.

## Why it stands out

Homecomin' succeeds because it is both:

- a crowd-pleasing comfort-food restaurant
- and a genuine destination meal for people who care about Disney Springs
  dining specifically

DFBGuide also flags the **weekend Rise and Shine brunch** as part of the
restaurant's identity, not just a side menu.

## Reservation pressure

Homecomin' is hard to book. DFBGuide explicitly notes that reservations here
can be difficult because everybody knows it is one of the strongest Disney
Springs options.

## Budget alternative

If the core craving is fried chicken at a lower price point, DFBGuide's swap
is **Chicken Guy**. It is not the same experience, but it is the cheaper way
into that general lane.

## Worth it for

- guests prioritizing one standout Disney Springs meal
- southern-comfort-food fans
- brunch-focused non-park days
