# Wiki Index

Content-oriented catalog. Updated on every ingest. The trip planner reads
this first to find relevant pages, then drills into specific files.

## Parks

- [magic-kingdom.md](magic-kingdom.md) — Flagship WDW park. Full state
  for 2026: closures, construction, lands, attractions, dining,
  entertainment, events, character meet locations.
- [epcot.md](epcot.md) — WDW's most flexible park. Least construction-
  dominated of the four; strong front-vs-back entrance strategy,
  festival identity, and mixed ride + dining planning.
- [animal-kingdom.md](animal-kingdom.md) — Construction-era 5-ride
  state; rides-first half-day logic, but still viable as a slower
  trails / shows / dining day.
- [hollywood-studios.md](hollywood-studios.md) — Film-and-TV park. In
  heavy 2026 construction: Rock 'n' Roller Muppets overlay, Walt
  Disney Studios lot opening, Millennium Falcon update, Monstropolis
  in progress.

## Districts and non-park areas

- [disney-springs.md](disney-springs.md) — Free Disney shopping,
  dining, and entertainment district. Useful for non-park days,
  rainy-day pivots, and food-first itineraries.

## Attractions

### Magic Kingdom

- [tron-lightcycle-run.md](tron-lightcycle-run.md) — Most popular
  Tomorrowland coaster. Single Pass only; peak demand post-sunset
  for lit grid canopy.
- [seven-dwarfs-mine-train.md](seven-dwarfs-mine-train.md) —
  Fantasyland family coaster. Single Pass only; one of top rope-drop
  priorities.
- [haunted-mansion.md](haunted-mansion.md) — Liberty Square classic
  dark ride. Tier 2 Multi Pass. Drops near fireworks.
- [tianas-bayou-adventure.md](tianas-bayou-adventure.md) — Frontierland
  flume ride. Tier 1 Multi Pass. The only active Frontierland
  headliner until Big Thunder reopens.
- [peter-pans-flight.md](peter-pans-flight.md) — Fantasyland suspended
  dark ride. Tier 1 Multi Pass but accessible during Early Theme Park
  Entry.
- [jungle-cruise.md](jungle-cruise.md) — Adventureland boat ride. Tier
  1 Multi Pass. Adventureland bottleneck warning for rope drop.
- [pirates-of-the-caribbean.md](pirates-of-the-caribbean.md) —
  Adventureland classic dark boat ride. Tier 2 Multi Pass.
- [buzz-lightyear-space-ranger-spin.md](buzz-lightyear-space-ranger-spin.md)
  — Tomorrowland interactive dark ride. Officially reopened early
  April 2026 with rebuilt gameplay. Tier 2 Multi Pass.
- [big-thunder-mountain-railroad.md](big-thunder-mountain-railroad.md)
  — Frontierland mine-train coaster. Reopening May 3, 2026 after
  major refurbishment. Height requirement lowered to 38".

### EPCOT

- [guardians-of-the-galaxy-cosmic-rewind.md](guardians-of-the-galaxy-cosmic-rewind.md)
  — EPCOT's hardest reservation and only Single Pass attraction.
- [frozen-ever-after.md](frozen-ever-after.md) — Norway pavilion boat
  ride. Tier 1 Multi Pass; early-2026 animatronic-upgrade closure.
- [remys-ratatouille-adventure.md](remys-ratatouille-adventure.md) —
  France pavilion trackless dark ride. Tier 1 Multi Pass; ideal
  International Gateway rope-drop target.
- [test-track.md](test-track.md) — Front-half thrill ride. Tier 1 Multi
  Pass; single rider available.
- [mission-space.md](mission-space.md) — Split-case simulator. Green is
  mild; Orange is one of the most intense rides in Disney World.

### Disney Springs

- [aerophile.md](aerophile.md) — Tethered balloon ride above Disney
  Springs. Great on clear days; fully weather dependent.

### Animal Kingdom

- [bluey-world.md](bluey-world.md) — Upcoming walkthrough attraction
  at Conservation Station / Rafiki's Planet Watch. Opens 2026-05-26.
- [avatar-flight-of-passage.md](avatar-flight-of-passage.md) — Animal
  Kingdom's hardest standby line and the park's smartest one-ride paid
  line-skip.
- [kilimanjaro-safaris.md](kilimanjaro-safaris.md) — Africa safari
  truck ride through a live animal reserve. Educational narration.
- [zootopia-better-zoogether.md](zootopia-better-zoogether.md) — New
  3D/4D theater show in the Tree of Life replacing "It's Tough to
  Be a Bug."
- [pandora-world-of-avatar.md](pandora-world-of-avatar.md) — Themed
  land with Flight of Passage and Na'vi River Journey. Nighttime
  black-light experience is distinct.
- [tropical-americas.md](tropical-americas.md) — Future Animal Kingdom
  land replacing DinoLand U.S.A. Construction only in 2026; later-
  2027 opening expected.

### Hollywood Studios

- [slinky-dog-dash.md](slinky-dog-dash.md) — Toy Story Land family
  coaster and one of the park's most important Multi Pass targets.
- [star-wars-rise-of-the-resistance.md](star-wars-rise-of-the-resistance.md)
  — Galaxy's Edge headliner. Major Lightning Lane tradeoff ride.
- [rock-n-roller-coaster.md](rock-n-roller-coaster.md) — Sunset
  Boulevard launch coaster. Closed for Muppets overlay, reopening
  summer 2026.
- [walt-disney-studios-lot.md](walt-disney-studios-lot.md) — New area
  replacing Animation Courtyard, opening later 2026. Magic of Disney
  Animation, Olaf-led Animation Academy, Disney Junior show.
- [millennium-falcon-smugglers-run.md](millennium-falcon-smugglers-run.md)
  — Galaxy's Edge interactive attraction. New mission with Mandalorian
  and Grogu launches May 22, 2026.
- [monstropolis.md](monstropolis.md) — Future Monsters Inc.-themed
  land in former Muppets Courtyard. Not opening until 2027-2028.

## Dining

- [cinderellas-royal-table.md](cinderellas-royal-table.md) — Signature
  prefix dining inside Cinderella Castle. Hardest MK ADR; princess
  meal. $76–89/adult.
- [be-our-guest.md](be-our-guest.md) — Signature prefix dining in
  Beast's castle, Fantasyland. "Paying for atmosphere, not food."
  $72/adult.
- [crystal-palace.md](crystal-palace.md) — Main Street buffet character
  meal with the Winnie the Pooh crew. Easier ADR than Cinderella's
  Royal Table.
- [skipper-canteen.md](skipper-canteen.md) — Adventureland adventurous
  global cuisine with secret menu. DFBGuide's pick for adventurous
  eaters.
- [liberty-tree-tavern.md](liberty-tree-tavern.md) — Liberty Square
  family-style "endless Thanksgiving" feast.
- [beacon-barrel.md](beacon-barrel.md) — New Pirates-themed interactive
  lounge in Adventureland. 45-minute time limit. Hottest new 60-day
  ADR target.
- [raglan-road.md](raglan-road.md) — Disney Springs Irish pub and live-
  entertainment destination. Strongest during festival periods.
- [homecomin.md](homecomin.md) — Southern comfort-food destination at
  Disney Springs. Hard reservation, strong brunch identity.
- [morimoto-asia.md](morimoto-asia.md) — Disney Springs signature pan-
  Asian restaurant with unusually broad menu coverage.
- [wine-bar-george.md](wine-bar-george.md) — Disney Springs wine-first
  destination led by a master sommelier.
- [jaleo.md](jaleo.md) — Disney Springs Spanish tapas and paella
  restaurant. Occasion-style shared-plate dinner.
- [gideons-bakehouse.md](gideons-bakehouse.md) — Tiny gothic bakery with
  giant cookies, cake, and lines that require timing strategy.
- [akershus-royal-banquet-hall.md](akershus-royal-banquet-hall.md) —
  Norway pavilion princess meal. Best value case at breakfast.
- [garden-grill.md](garden-grill.md) — The Land pavilion rotating
  character meal. Strong sit-down value and one of AJ's favorites.
- [space-220.md](space-220.md) — Highly immersive EPCOT restaurant.
  Atmosphere-forward splurge; lounge is the smarter play than full meal.
- [satuli-canteen.md](satuli-canteen.md) — Pandora (Animal Kingdom)
  build-your-own-bowl quick service. One of the best Disney
  quick-service restaurants on property.
- [tusker-house.md](tusker-house.md) — Animal Kingdom character buffet.
  Best current table-service Disney Dining Plan value in the park.
- [nomad-lounge.md](nomad-lounge.md) — Discovery Island lounge attached
  to Tiffins. Best slower-paced adult break in Animal Kingdom.

## Hotels

- [disney-world-hotels.md](disney-world-hotels.md) — Hotel-planning hub:
  tiers, transportation tradeoffs, best-fit resort buckets, and future
  hotel to watch.

### Value

- [all-star-resorts.md](all-star-resorts.md) — Disney's cheapest hotel
  tier. Music has the key family-suite edge; Sports often has the best
  bus-loop position.
- [art-of-animation-resort.md](art-of-animation-resort.md) — Most
  aggressively themed value resort. Strong family suites, pools, and
  Skyliner access.
- [pop-century-resort.md](pop-century-resort.md) — Cheapest Skyliner
  hotel. One of the strongest budget-to-useful hotel plays on property.

### Moderate

- [caribbean-beach-resort.md](caribbean-beach-resort.md) — Huge moderate
  with Skyliner upside, but room location matters a lot.
- [coronado-springs-resort.md](coronado-springs-resort.md) — Moderate
  with a split identity: regular outer buildings vs the much more polished
  Gran Destino Tower.
- [port-orleans-resort.md](port-orleans-resort.md) — New Orleans-themed
  moderate with Riverside + French Quarter sections, boat access to
  Disney Springs, and updated Tiana rooms.

### Deluxe / DVC

- [animal-kingdom-lodge.md](animal-kingdom-lodge.md) — Savanna hotel
  with 200+ animals, strong dining, and destination-worthy resort-day
  energy.
- [beach-club-resort.md](beach-club-resort.md) — Closest hotel to EPCOT's
  International Gateway. Stormalong Bay is the major draw.
- [boardwalk-inn.md](boardwalk-inn.md) — EPCOT-area boardwalk hotel with
  day-to-night personality shift and stronger nightlife energy.
- [contemporary-resort.md](contemporary-resort.md) — Magic Kingdom walk +
  Monorail access. One of the most practical deluxe splurges.
- [grand-floridian-resort.md](grand-floridian-resort.md) — Disney's
  flagship luxury hotel. Victorian styling, high-end dining, Magic
  Kingdom convenience.
- [old-key-west-resort.md](old-key-west-resort.md) — Relaxed Florida-Keys
  DVC resort with large villas and Disney Springs boat access.
- [polynesian-village-resort.md](polynesian-village-resort.md) — Tropical
  Magic Kingdom-area icon with great food, fireworks views, and the
  overwater bungalow splurge.
- [riviera-resort.md](riviera-resort.md) — Most expensive Skyliner resort.
  Elegant, quiet, and Topolino's-driven.
- [saratoga-springs-resort.md](saratoga-springs-resort.md) — Disney
  Springs-adjacent DVC resort with Treehouse Villas and a mixed motel-
  feel / location-value tradeoff.
- [wilderness-lodge.md](wilderness-lodge.md) — Cozy Pacific Northwest
  deluxe resort with boat access to Magic Kingdom.
- [yacht-club-resort.md](yacht-club-resort.md) — More formal sibling to
  Beach Club. Same EPCOT-area advantages, different tone.

### Specialty / outdoor

- [fort-wilderness-resort.md](fort-wilderness-resort.md) — Cabins,
  campground, boats to Magic Kingdom, and the strongest outdoor-activity
  lineup of any Disney resort.

### Future hotel

- [lakeshore-lodge.md](lakeshore-lodge.md) — New Disney resort expected
  in 2027. Not a 2026 trip option yet.
