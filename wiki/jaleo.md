# Jaleo

Disney Springs signature Spanish restaurant from José Andrés, built around
shareable tapas and larger-format paella. One of DFBGuide's preferred higher-
end Disney Springs meals for groups who want a dinner that feels like an
occasion.

## Why it stands out

Jaleo is not just a place to eat one dish and leave. The intended rhythm is:

- multiple shareable plates
- strong beverage pairings
- a more social style of dining than standard individual-entrée restaurants

DFBGuide specifically flags the paella as a favorite move.

## Budget alternative

**Pepe** is the lower-cost testing ground for Jaleo. It gives you a much
cheaper way to sample the cuisine and decide whether the full restaurant is a
future priority.

## Worth it for

- groups who like sharing plates
- adults-only Disney Springs dinners
- guests who want something more distinctive than standard steak / burger /
  chicken Disney dining
