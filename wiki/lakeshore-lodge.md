# Lakeshore Lodge

Future Disney hotel expected to open in **2027**.
Not a 2026 trip option, but important as the next major Walt Disney World
resort opening.

## What it is

DFBGuide describes Lakeshore Lodge as a new **Disney Vacation Club villa
resort** that will still be bookable by non-members, just like Disney's
other DVC properties.

It was originally expected to open much earlier under the abandoned
**Reflections** concept, but is now back on track under the Lakeshore
Lodge name.

## What to expect

DFBGuide's current read:

- nature-focused design
- nods to films like **Bambi**, **Brother Bear**, **Pocahontas**, and
  **The Princess and the Frog**
- a substantial **lazy river** at the main pool area
- a **waterfront restaurant**

## Trip-planning relevance

For 2026 trips: ignore this as a booking target.
For 2027+ planning: this is one of the most important future resort
openings to watch.
