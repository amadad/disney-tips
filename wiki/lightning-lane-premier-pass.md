# Lightning Lane Premier Pass

Disney's highest-priced line-skipping product.
Built for guests who want the simplest possible version of Lightning
Lane: **show up, scan in, ride once** at every eligible attraction in a
single park.

## What it is

Premier Pass covers both:

- participating **Multi Pass attractions**
- participating **Single Pass attractions**

You do **not** make advance ride selections throughout the day the way
you do with Multi Pass. You simply walk up to each eligible attraction,
scan in, and use that attraction's Lightning Lane **once**.

## Biggest limits

- **one park per day only**
- **not Park Hopper-friendly**
- **very expensive** — DFBGuide frames it as a hundreds-of-dollars-per-
  person, per-day purchase

This is what makes Premier Pass a niche product. It is not the flexible
budget-friendly move; it is the "I do not want to think about lines"
move.

## Best for

- single-park splurge days
- guests who hate app micromanagement
- high-budget trips where minimizing standby time matters more than cost

## Usually not best for

- guests park-hopping between multiple parks in one day
- travelers trying to maximize value per dollar
- parties who can manage Multi Pass strategy without much stress

## Better alternative for most guests

For most trips, [lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md)
will be the better value. It is cheaper, works better with park-hopping,
and still lets you skip a meaningful amount of standby time if you know
how to use it well.
