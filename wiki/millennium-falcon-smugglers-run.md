# Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run

Hollywood Studios Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge interactive attraction.
Guests board the Millennium Falcon in six-person crews (pilots,
gunners, engineers) and fly a mission through the Star Wars universe.
The flagship hands-on experience at Galaxy's Edge.

## May 22, 2026: new mission update

On **May 22, 2026**, the attraction is getting a significant content
update:

- **New name**: "Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run — A New Mission"
  (per MickeyViews 2026-04)
- **New storyline**: features **Mandalorian** (Din Djarin) and
  **Grogu** joining the crew during the adventure
- **Choose your destination**: guests will be able to select where
  the Falcon goes in the Star Wars universe during the mission.
  Confirmed destination options include:
  - **Bespin**
  - **The wreckage of the second Death Star**
  - **Endor**
  - **Coruscant**

  Or some combination thereof during a single mission run. This was
  part of the original vision promised for the attraction when it
  first opened.

## Rumored engineer role update

**Unconfirmed rumor** per MickeyViews 2026-04: Josh D'Amaro (Chairman
of Disney Experiences) has reportedly been pushing for the engineer
roles on the attraction to be **more active and fun**. Currently
engineers sit in the back and have substantially less to do than
gunners or pilots. Rumor suggests engineers may be the ones who
**select the destination** during the new mission — but this is
not officially confirmed.

## No gameplay overhaul otherwise

The core mechanics of the attraction remain unchanged. Guests still
fly the Falcon; pilots still pilot, gunners still gun. The update is
additive storyline and destination content — not a mechanical rebuild
like [Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin](buzz-lightyear-space-ranger-spin.md)
at Magic Kingdom.

## Walt Disney World is not getting Disneyland's other Galaxy's Edge updates (yet)

Disneyland's Galaxy's Edge is getting two *additional* updates as of
**April 29, 2026** that Walt Disney World has not yet announced:

- **Expanded timeline** — Darth Vader, Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, and
  Leia returning to the land, pulling the story back to the original
  trilogy (on top of the sequel trilogy characters that are currently
  present)
- **John Williams music playing throughout the land** — the famous
  Star Wars themes audible throughout Galaxy's Edge, not just inside
  attractions

WDW's Galaxy's Edge remains strictly in the sequel timeline and keeps
its quieter, sparer sound design. MickeyViews hopes both updates come
to WDW soon but notes no announcement has been made.

## Trip planning

- **Trips before May 22, 2026**: ride the attraction as currently
  configured. Expect standard Smugglers Run experience with existing
  mission content.
- **Trips May 22, 2026 onward**: plan to ride it at least twice —
  once to experience the new mission, once to explore a different
  destination option. Expect high demand during the opening window.
- The attraction is **not** getting a forced closure for the update —
  it continues operating through the transition.
