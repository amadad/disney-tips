# Monstropolis

Future Hollywood Studios themed land based on the **Monsters, Inc.**
film universe. Located on the footprint of the **former Muppets
Courtyard**. **Not opening in 2026** — MickeyViews 2026-04 speculates
**2027 or 2028** as the realistic opening window. Construction is
actively underway but behind construction walls; little is visible
from guest areas except for the former Muppet Vision 3D theater's
facade work.

## What will be in it

### Monsters Inc. Door Factory Coaster (e-ticket headliner)

The planned **e-ticket attraction** for the land — a suspended roller
coaster themed around the monsters' door-factory scare floor from
*Monsters, Inc.*

**Construction method note** (engineer-trivia from MickeyViews): this
coaster's foundation is being built with a different method than the
previous generation of WDW coasters. Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic
Rewind and Tron Lightcycle Run were built with pre-poured rebar +
conduit in one massive foundation pour. The Monsters Inc. coaster is
being built with a **"sandwich slab" method** — layered foundation
with sand packing between concrete layers. Engineers in MickeyViews's
audience suggest this is a new Disney approach to coaster
foundations. Implications for ride experience or construction speed
are unclear.

### Glob Theater (Mike Wazowski attraction)

Replacing the **former Muppet Vision 3D theater**. Themed around
**Mike Wazowski** — MickeyViews suggests the name "Glob Theater"
implies a Shakespeare/Globe Theater reference (consistent with Mike's
comedic-performer personality in the Monsters Inc. universe). Format
not yet confirmed; likely either a show, a simulator, or an
interactive attraction.

Disney is **visibly demolishing and rebuilding the facade** of the
former Muppet Vision 3D theater during park hours in early 2026 —
removing brick tiles from the water-barrier substrate and adding new
facade elements. MickeyViews reads this as Disney signaling
"Monstropolis is a real new land, not just a repaint of what was
here before."

### Dining and shopping

- A **sushi restaurant** is planned inside the former Muppets
  Courtyard footprint
- Additional shopping and dining locations
- A central open courtyard area for guests to relax and eat — a
  similar design approach to the new
  [Walt Disney Studios lot](walt-disney-studios-lot.md) courtyard
  elsewhere in the park

## Opening timeline

- **2026**: construction only. Not guest-accessible.
- **2027 or 2028 (speculated)**: land opens. No firm date. Disney has
  not publicly committed to a year.

**MickeyViews's tempo framing**: Monstropolis and Animal Kingdom's
Tropical Americas are Disney's current "actively moving" park
expansion projects. Both are noticeably faster in construction pace
than the drawn-out Tron and Guardians of the Galaxy builds, which
became famous for slow timelines.

## Where the Muppets went

The former Muppets Courtyard hosted **Muppet Vision 3D** (now
closed) and the Muppets themed dining/shopping. The Muppets are not
leaving Hollywood Studios — they are migrating to the
[Rock 'n' Roller Coaster overlay](rock-n-roller-coaster.md), where
The Electric Mayhem (Animal's band) is replacing Aerosmith as the
ride's featured act. This is a deliberate Disney move to keep the
Muppets in the park while reclaiming their former land for Monsters
Inc.

## Trip planning

**Do not include in 2026 trip plans.** The land is not accessible
and there is no Monstropolis-related guest content open. Mention
only when planning far-future trips (2027 at the earliest, more
likely 2028).

For 2026 trips to Hollywood Studios, the Muppets Courtyard is
walled off and visually just a hard-hat area. Plan the day around
the current operating attractions; see
[hollywood-studios.md](hollywood-studios.md).
