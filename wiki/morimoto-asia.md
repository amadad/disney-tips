# Morimoto Asia

Disney Springs signature restaurant serving pan-Asian cuisine across a very
large menu. DFBGuide treats it as one of the district's true destination
restaurants because of the breadth of the food, the waterfront setting, and
the fact that the menu can support very different appetites at once.

## Why it stands out

Morimoto Asia offers:

- sushi
- dim sum
- noodle and rice dishes
- premium meat options including A5 Wagyu
- specialty drinks
- vegetarian options

That breadth is part of the appeal. It makes Morimoto easier to justify for
mixed groups than a narrower specialty restaurant.

## Budget alternative

DFBGuide's explicit cheaper alternative is **Morimoto Asia Street Food**,
located outside on the patio. The food is more limited, but it preserves part
of the core flavor profile without the signature-restaurant spend.

## Worth it for

- mixed groups with varied tastes
- diners who want a true Disney Springs signature dinner
- guests looking for a step up from standard park dining
