# Old Key West Resort

Laid-back Disney Vacation Club resort themed around the Florida Keys.
Best for guests who want **space**, a relaxed community feel, and easy
boat access to Disney Springs.

## Why it stands out

Old Key West was Disney's **first Disney Vacation Club resort**, and
DFBGuide's most important practical claim is about the room sizes:
its villas are among the **largest on property**.

## What the resort feels like

- palm trees
- hammocks
- sandy / Florida-Keys atmosphere
- slower, neighborhood-style pacing
- a stronger sense of returning guests / DVC regulars

## Dining and access

DFBGuide specifically calls out:

- **Olivia's Cafe**
- boat access to [Disney Springs](disney-springs.md)
- the resort's key-lime Dole Whip as a signature snack move

## Room formats

- deluxe studios
- one-bedroom villas
- two-bedroom villas
- three-bedroom villas

## Best for

- larger parties that need more space
- Disney Springs-adjacent trips
- guests who want a calm, non-flashy resort day
