# Peter Pan's Flight

Magic Kingdom Fantasyland suspended dark ride. Guests fly over London
and Neverland in pirate ships. Beloved for its scenic charm; cursed by
a tiny capacity that produces chronic 60+ minute waits despite being
a short, slow ride.

## Lightning Lane

Peter Pan's Flight is a **Tier 1** Multi Pass attraction at Magic
Kingdom (along with Jungle Cruise, Space Mountain, and Tiana's Bayou
Adventure). Only one Tier 1 attraction can be pre-selected, so Peter
Pan competes for that slot.

**DFBGuide's guidance**: Peter Pan is often a weaker Tier 1 choice
*only because* it is accessible during Early Theme Park Entry — the
30-minute head start for Disney Resort Collection guests. If you
have early entry, you can potentially ride Peter Pan for free at
rope drop alongside [Seven Dwarfs Mine Train](seven-dwarfs-mine-train.md),
which frees up your Tier 1 slot for a more scarce choice (Tiana's or
Jungle Cruise). If you *don't* have early entry, Peter Pan becomes a
more legitimate Tier 1 pick.

Note: if you book a different Tier 1 pre-selection but see a
Peter Pan slot pop up in the My Disney Experience app later in the
day, grab it. Once you're inside the park, tier restrictions drop
and any available slot is fair game.

See [lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md).
