# Pirates of the Caribbean

Magic Kingdom Adventureland dark-ride boat attraction. Guests board a
boat and drift through animatronic pirate scenes, including the famous
town-siege scene and the ride's original "yo-ho" theme song. **The
*Pirates of the Caribbean* film franchise was based on this ride, not
the other way around** — the ride opened decades before the first film.

**Captain Jack Sparrow animatronics were added in 2006**, after the
first film's success, across several scenes in the ride. Watch for him
as you drift through — he's hidden in multiple spots.

## Wait dynamics

DFBGuide reports typical waits of **20–30 minutes** — lower than
[Jungle Cruise's](jungle-cruise.md) 30–50 minute average. DFBGuide
calls out the pattern: Pirates is similar to
[Haunted Mansion](haunted-mansion.md) in that waits fluctuate with
park-wide events (fireworks, dinner rush) rather than staying flat
all day. "Not terrible in Disney speak."

## Lightning Lane

Pirates of the Caribbean is a **Tier 2** Multi Pass attraction at
Magic Kingdom — it does *not* compete for the one Tier 1 pre-selection
slot (Tier 1 is Jungle Cruise, Space Mountain, Peter Pan, Tiana's).
This makes it a natural candidate for stacking as a secondary
pre-selection.

See [lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md).

## Adventureland context

Pirates anchors the Adventureland dining and attraction cluster. The
new [Beacon Barrel](beacon-barrel.md) interactive pirate lounge opened
nearby and is designed to expand the Pirates of the Caribbean
storyline — if you're planning a Pirates-themed half-day, the Barrel
is the must-book reservation to pair with the ride.
