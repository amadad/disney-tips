# Pop Century Resort

Value resort that solves one of the most common Disney hotel problems:
how to stay cheap **without giving up Skyliner access**.

## Why it stands out

DFBGuide's cleanest summary:
**Pop Century is the cheapest hotel on the Skyliner route.**

That is the whole point.
You get:

- value-resort pricing
- Skyliner access to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios
- fun oversized-decade theming

## The catch

Room location matters.
DFBGuide warns that if you are stuck deep in the **1980s / 1990s**
buildings, the walk to the Skyliner station can get pretty long.

That is why a **preferred room** may be worth the extra money.

## Food-court note

The Everything Pop food court is still value-resort dining, but DFBGuide
highlights a few memorable sweet items:

- giant ice-cream-sundae truck
- flashback oatmeal cream pie
- cake bar with freeze-dried Skittles
- tie-dye cheesecake

## Best for

- budget travelers who care about transportation efficiency
- EPCOT / Hollywood Studios-heavy trips
- guests who want the best cheap-to-useful hotel ratio in the Disney
  system
