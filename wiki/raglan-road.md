# Raglan Road

Disney Springs Irish pub and live-entertainment anchor.
One of the district's most reliable destination restaurants because it offers
something many Disney restaurants do not: **real atmosphere even if you are
not seated at the absolute best table**, thanks to the frequent live music and
Irish dance performances.

## Why it stands out

DFBGuide positions Raglan Road as one of the clearest Disney Springs
priorities because it combines:

- authentic Irish-pub identity
- strong food reputation
- frequent live entertainment
- major seasonal event programming

This makes it more than just a dinner reservation — it is often an evening
plan by itself.

## Seasonal event importance

Raglan Road becomes especially relevant during:

- **Mighty St. Patrick's Festival**
- **The Great Irish Hooley** around Labor Day

These are not casual background overlays. They are major live-entertainment
moments for the restaurant.

## Budget alternative

If you want the Irish-food lane without paying for full table service,
DFBGuide's explicit alternative is **Cooks of Dublin**, the nearby quick-
service counterpart run by the same ownership group.

## Worth it for

- guests who want dinner plus live entertainment
- adults-only or mixed-age groups looking for nightlife without a park ticket
- St. Patrick's / Irish-festival visitors
