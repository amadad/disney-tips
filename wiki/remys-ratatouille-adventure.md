# Remy's Ratatouille Adventure

France pavilion trackless dark ride in EPCOT's World Showcase.
One of the park's three **Tier 1 Lightning Lane Multi Pass** attractions
and one of the clearest examples of why EPCOT's back entrance matters.

## Why this ride is a planning target

Remy sits very close to **International Gateway**, the back entrance used
by guests walking from BoardWalk / Yacht Club / Beach Club / Swan /
Dolphin or arriving from the Skyliner resorts.

That means Remy becomes the most natural **rope-drop target** for guests
approaching EPCOT from that side of the park.

## Lightning Lane and single rider

- **Tier 1 Multi Pass** attraction
- **Single rider available**

DFBGuide explicitly calls out Remy's single-rider queue as one of the
better free wait-cutting tools in EPCOT. It may split your group, but it
can reduce the time cost enough to make the trade worthwhile.

## Practical implication

If your group is using International Gateway in the morning, Remy is one
of the best reasons to do it. If your group is entering from the front,
then [Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind](guardians-of-the-galaxy-cosmic-rewind.md)
and [Test Track](test-track.md) become stronger opening moves instead.
