# Rock 'n' Roller Coaster

Hollywood Studios Sunset Boulevard launch coaster. Historically themed
to **Aerosmith** — guests board stretch limousines and are launched
through a dark Los Angeles "studio lot" nighttime soundscape. As of
early 2026 the attraction is **closed for a Muppets-themed overlay**
and is expected to reopen in **summer 2026** (MickeyViews 2026-04:
"early summer" is the target).

## The overlay, not a full retheme

MickeyViews's on-location read: this is being treated as an **overlay**,
not a full attraction reimagining. The ride mechanics, track, limousine
vehicles, scene framework, and exterior guitar sculpture are all being
preserved. What is changing:

- **Theme and storyline**: swapped from Aerosmith's Los Angeles "get to
  the show" premise to a **Muppets** version featuring The Electric
  Mayhem (Animal's band), also racing across town to their own gig
- **Limousine vehicles**: repainted (keeping the vehicle design)
- **Ride scenes**: Tinsel Town decorations getting Muppets updates.
  Extent of the scene changes unclear as of April 2026
- **Exterior guitar**: staying but being repainted, with new signage
  replacing the old Aerosmith branding
- **Characters in the ride**: Muppets animatronics replacing Aerosmith
  elements

The ride name will change but the specific new name is not yet
confirmed — MickeyViews speculates something like "Rock 'n' Roller
Coaster Starring the Muppets."

## Construction timeline

- **2026-03-01**: final scheduled day of the Aerosmith version, per
  DFBGuide 2026-01
- **Early 2026 onward**: Ride closed for the overlay work
- **Summer 2026 (target)**: Reopening, per MickeyViews 2026-04 — "early
  summer, hopefully"

Disney is moving fast on this one: the Aerosmith signage was already
removed when MickeyViews filmed on 2026-04-09, and exterior changes
were visibly underway.

## Commemorative pressed pennies

Worth noting for collectors: MickeyViews flagged that Disney has
installed **new commemorative pressed pennies** on the machine near
the phase paint booth outside the attraction, marking the construction
period itself. These are currently available during the closure window
and — per MickeyViews — are specifically meant to commemorate the
overlay construction, not the new attraction when it opens.

## Trip planning

- **Trips through ~May 2026**: ride is closed. Do not plan it into
  a Hollywood Studios day.
- **Trips June 2026 onward**: check current status before your trip.
  Target is early summer but there is no firm opening date yet.
- **Trips during the reopening window**: expect high demand — this
  will be a newly-reopened attraction with substantial
  repeat-ride-value from returning guests who haven't experienced
  the Muppets version.

## Context: the Muppets are migrating

The Muppets' former home at Hollywood Studios was **Muppet Vision
3D** in the now-closed Muppets Courtyard. That courtyard is being
transformed into [Monstropolis](monstropolis.md), a Monsters Inc.
land. Rock 'n' Roller Coaster is effectively the Muppets' new
flagship attraction at Hollywood Studios — they're not leaving the
park, they're moving buildings.
