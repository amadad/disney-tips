# Saratoga Springs Resort

Disney Vacation Club resort near Disney Springs with a horse-racing /
upstate-New-York country-club identity. Best understood as a space-and-
location play rather than a deluxe wow-factor play.

## Why it stands out

The main real-world advantages are:

- **walking path to Disney Springs** from some sections
- boat access to [Disney Springs](disney-springs.md)
- relatively lower deluxe pricing compared with some other deluxe resorts
- the unusual **Treehouse Villas**

## Treehouse Villas

These are the resort's signature room type:

- sleep up to **9 guests**
- private-deck feeling
- more secluded / woodsier placement

But DFBGuide flags two planning issues:

- they are hard to book
- renovations are ongoing through **mid-September 2026**, so existing
  stays may include daytime construction nearby and there are fewer
  available treehouses at the moment

## The tradeoff

DFBGuide is unusually blunt about Saratoga's weaknesses:

- no spa anymore
- dining is relatively weak
- some room sections feel more motel-like than deluxe
- your room can be **very far** from the main amenities or Disney Springs

## Best for

- Disney Springs-heavy trips
- larger groups who want Treehouse Villas
- guests looking for a sometimes-cheaper entry into deluxe-tier lodging
