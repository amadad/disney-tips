# Slinky Dog Dash

Toy Story Land family coaster and one of Hollywood Studios's most
important Lightning Lane priorities.

## Why it matters

Slinky Dog Dash is one of the park's most valuable Multi Pass targets.
DFBGuide 2026-04 uses it as the clearest example of a ride worth doing
**twice in two different ways** if your day allows it.

## Lightning Lane

Slinky Dog Dash is treated as a **Tier 1 Multi Pass priority** in the
source. If you pre-select it, you are using one of your most valuable
advanced reservations on a genuine headliner.

## Ride-twice strategy

DFBGuide's specific move:

1. use **Early Theme Park Entry** to ride Slinky Dog in standby first
2. use a **later Lightning Lane** to ride it again at night

Why this works:

- you avoid the worst standby waits in the morning
- you still get a second ride later without paying that queue cost again
- the ride feels different at night, which makes the second ride more
  than a duplicate

## Tradeoff

Once you use a Lightning Lane on Slinky Dog Dash, you cannot keep
collecting new Slinky Lightning Lanes all day. So if you want multiple
rides, the cleanest move is **standby first, Lightning Lane later**.
