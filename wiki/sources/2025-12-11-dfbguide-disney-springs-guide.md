## Brief

DFBGuide's broad planning guide to Disney Springs in 2026. This source
**opens Disney Springs as a major non-park planning area in the wiki** and is
important because it shifts the frame from "shopping district" to
"destination Disney day." It covers logistics (parking, security,
transportation, crowd timing), seasonal programming, dining priorities,
snacks, attractions, and practical ways to use Disney Springs as either a
budget-flexible day or a rainy-day fallback.
