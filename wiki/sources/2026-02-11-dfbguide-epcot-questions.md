## Brief

DFBGuide's broad planning guide to EPCOT in early 2026. This source
**opens EPCOT as a full park in the wiki** and answers several open
questions that had been hanging since the first AllEars ingest — most
notably the **EPCOT Lightning Lane tier list**. It is especially useful
for trip-planner decisions rather than fandom trivia: whether EPCOT needs
one day or two, when Multi Pass is worth buying, whether the front or
back entrance matters, which rides are actually stressful, and which
restaurants are worth major budget or reservation energy.
