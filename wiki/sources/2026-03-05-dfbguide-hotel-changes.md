## Strategic pattern reinforced

The hotel layer is not just about picking the right resort once.
It also needs **operational state tracking**: what is closed, what is
under construction, what policy changed, what perk is still valid, and
whether a marquee experience like fireworks at 'Ohana is currently worth
booking around.
