## Brief

A broad source about the places Disney can feel like a **bad deal even
when nothing is technically wrong**: same-priced hotel rooms with worse
locations, bad restaurant tables, construction-hidden views, shortened
full-price park days, weather-gutted party nights, convenience pricing,
MagicBand upsells, Park Hopper Plus math, and Lightning Lane purchased too
late to be useful.

This source matters because it directly attacks one of the strongest
product pain points: **value anxiety / rip-off avoidance**.

## Strategic pattern reinforced

**A Disney rip-off is often just a mismatch between what the guest thinks
they are buying and what they are actually getting.** This source pushes
that idea across rooms, restaurants, add-ons, tickets, and events.
