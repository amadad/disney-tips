## Brief

Broad Animal Kingdom Q&A framed around the park's **construction-era
identity crisis** in 2026. This source matters because it sharpens the
current Animal Kingdom thesis: the park is genuinely reduced for
rides-first travelers, but it is **not** empty if you care about trails,
shows, characters, dining, and a slower pace. It also opens the next
major future-state page for the park via
[Tropical Americas](../tropical-americas.md).

## Strategic pattern reinforced

**"Animal Kingdom is a half-day park" needs qualification.** It is true
for parties who care mostly about rides and line efficiency. It is much
less true for parties willing to use trails, shows, rare characters,
shopping, and destination dining as part of the park day. That nuance now
lives in `animal-kingdom.md` and `strategy-animal-kingdom-half-day.md`.
