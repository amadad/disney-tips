## Brief

A broad source on the mistakes people make **once the trip is already in
motion**: wasting Early Theme Park Entry by arriving too late, not
understanding transportation cutoffs, assuming resort hopping means free
parking and pool access, mishandling Mobile Order, underestimating hidden
costs, packing the wrong shoes, and accidentally ruining EPCOT with back-
to-back motion-sickness rides.

This source matters because it tightens the wiki's **operational layer**.
It is not mainly about what Disney offers. It is about how guests lose
value through friction, timing mistakes, and wrong assumptions.

## Strategic pattern reinforced

**Disney friction compounds when guests assume the system works the way
other vacations work.** This source reinforces that the product should
not just recommend attractions and restaurants — it should also warn
people about the operational mistakes that quietly waste the trip.
