## Brief

A scenario-driven source built around four real Disney problems:
getting headliners without Lightning Lane, recovering a hot / cranky
mood, handling a thunderstorm without wasting the day, and salvaging a
park day after learning the park closes early.

This source matters because it turns abstract advice into **worked
examples** of how to pivot inside a live park day.

## Strategic pattern reinforced

**Many Disney problems do not need premium add-ons. They need clean
pivots.** This source reinforces that timing, indoor shelters, snacks,
hydration, and willingness to leave / reset can solve problems that many
first-timers assume require more money.
