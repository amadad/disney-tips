## Brief

A broad source on how to lower the total cost of a Disney World trip by
stacking several non-glamorous decisions correctly: low-demand weeks,
special ticket products, hotel tier choice, included-food hotels,
promotion math, grocery strategy, free water, and midweek flight timing.

This source matters because it is not just "cheap Disney" clickbait. It
explains that the strongest savings usually come from **trip structure**,
not one-off hacks.

## Strategic pattern reinforced

**Budget Disney is mostly demand management and constraint matching.**
The cheapest trip is rarely the one with the smallest visible room rate.
It is the one where dates, tickets, hotel, food, and transportation all
stop fighting one another.
