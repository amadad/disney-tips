## Brief

Full first-person tour of Disney's Port Orleans Resort during a
resort-only stay. Covers the recently-updated Tiana-themed rooms in
Magnolia Bend's Oak Manor, the resort's full amenity set (fishing
hole, horse carriage rides, pools, live entertainment, campfire),
Boatwright's "Taste of the Bayou" $38 family-style dinner, Riverside
Mill quick-service New Orleans cuisine, and the French Quarter's
Mickey beignets. **Opens the hotels category in the wiki** —
previously zero resort coverage.
