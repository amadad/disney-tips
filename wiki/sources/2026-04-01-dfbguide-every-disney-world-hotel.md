## Strategic pattern reinforced

**Hotel choice is a product input, not just logistics.** This source
sharpens the product thesis from `docs/product-pain-points.md`: guests do
not just need "tips." They need help choosing the hotel that matches how
they actually want to move through the trip — cheapest room, cheapest
Skyliner room, best family suite, best EPCOT access, best Magic Kingdom
access, strongest resort-day, strongest dining, or easiest Disney Springs
adjacency.

## Page-count discipline

Aggressive but justified. This source was broad enough to earn a hotel hub
plus a substantial set of hotel entity pages because the hotel layer is a
major planning axis, not side detail. We still kept some discipline:
there are no standalone pages yet for Chef Mickey's, Topolino's, Beaches &
Cream, Trader Sam's, Stormalong Bay, Gran Destino Tower, Bay Lake Tower,
Copper Creek, Boulder Ridge, Island Tower, Treehouse Villas, or club
level as separate concepts. Those remain embedded until future sources
corroborate or deepen them.
