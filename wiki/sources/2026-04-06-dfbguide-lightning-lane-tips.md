## Strategic pattern reinforced

**The product is not just "what Lightning Lane is." It is how to make
tradeoffs under real park pressure.** This source pushes the wiki from
reference toward usable trip-planning mechanics: where to spend money,
where not to, how to turn Early Entry and park hopping into leverage,
and how to keep app friction from wrecking the day.
