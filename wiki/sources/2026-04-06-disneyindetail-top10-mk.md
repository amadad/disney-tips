## Contributed factoids (new to wiki)

- **Tron Lightcycle Run opened 2023**
- **Tiana's Bayou Adventure opened 2024** as a reimagining of Splash
  Mountain
- **Seven Dwarfs Mine Train opened 2014**
- **Tron: no inversions, accessible seats available, mandatory
  lockering** (because speed flings loose items)
- **Seven Dwarfs Mine Train: 38" height requirement**, good starter
  coaster for young kids
- **Pirates of the Caribbean: films based on the ride**, not the
  other way around
- **Pirates of the Caribbean: Captain Jack Sparrow animatronics added
  in 2006**
- **Jungle Cruise and Haunted Mansion are opening-day attractions**
  (continuously operating since 1971)
- **Space Mountain has a Christmas overlay during Mickey's Very Merry
  Christmas Party nights** — the attraction's normal dark-ride
  atmosphere changes and the track becomes partially visible under
  the modified lighting (captured for MK hub page update)
- **Tiana's Bayou Adventure can drop to 5-minute waits** on cool or
  rainy days when guests skip to avoid getting soaked
- **Halloween Party and Christmas Party nights**: Magic Kingdom closes
  to general admission at 6 PM; regular fireworks and nighttime
  parade do not run for general guests on those nights
- **Festival of Fantasy typical showtime**: ~3 PM
- **Disney Starlight nightly parade**: typically 8 PM and 10 PM, or
  9 PM and 11 PM; reduces to once per night in colder quieter months
- **Happily Ever After viewing**: Contemporary Resort / Bay Lake
  Tower bridge is a valid off-property viewing spot
