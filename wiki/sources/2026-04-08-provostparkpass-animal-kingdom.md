## Strategic patterns reinforced

- **Do not use Lightning Lane Multi Pass on theater shows** —
  reinforced twice in this source (Lion King and Zootopia). This is
  a pattern the trip planner should apply across parks, not just AK.
- **Satu'li Canteen is a destination-worthy AK lunch stop** — fits
  the strategy-animal-kingdom-half-day.md recommendation of using AK
  as a starter park with at least one meal on property before
  park-hopping out
