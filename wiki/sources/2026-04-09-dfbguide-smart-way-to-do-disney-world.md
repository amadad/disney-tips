## Brief

A wide but surprisingly useful source about how Disney planning changes
based on **who is in the group**: low-patience adults, babies, penny
pinchers, foodies, heat-fried families, sensory-sensitive travelers,
older adults, guests with special diets, completionists, and people who
hate using their phones all day.

This source matters because it helps translate the wiki's growing park /
hotel / dining / ticket knowledge into a more product-relevant question:
**what kind of party am I planning for?**

## Strategic pattern reinforced

**Party fit is a first-class planning variable.** The best Disney advice
changes meaningfully depending on whether the trip is constrained by
patience, naps, budget, sensory load, mobility, food restrictions, or
app tolerance.

## Page-count discipline

Light-to-moderate. This source earned one new strategy page because the
corpus now has enough signal that **party-specific planning** should be
retrievable directly, not left scattered across park, hotel, dining, and
operational pages.

It did **not** earn separate pages yet for Baby Care Centers, ECV rental,
Special Diets, My Disney Experience as a standalone concept, or
sensory-sensitive ride lists.
