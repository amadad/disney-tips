## Topics covered

1. Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Aerosmith → Muppets overlay (not full
   retheme), summer 2026 reopening target
2. Animation Courtyard → Walt Disney Studios lot rebrand with updated
   Magic of Disney Animation, controversial Olaf-animatronic Animation
   Academy, Disney Junior show port from DCA, new courtyard park area
3. Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run mission update on May 22 2026 with
   Mandalorian/Grogu storyline and choose-destination gameplay
4. Disneyland-only Galaxy's Edge timeline expansion and John Williams
   music (WDW not getting these yet)
5. Monstropolis land construction — Monsters Inc. Door Factory Coaster
   (e-ticket, novel "sandwich slab" foundation), Glob Theater, sushi
   restaurant, opens 2027-2028
6. The Muppets' migration from the former Muppets Courtyard to Rock 'n'
   Roller Coaster
7. New Toy Story Land popcorn stand with Pizza Planet spring rolls
8. Jedi Training Academy still closed since 2020, not reopening,
   creator editorial argument for its return
