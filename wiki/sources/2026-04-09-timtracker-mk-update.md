## Topics covered

1. Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin officially reopened — ride
   operational, animatronic uncurtained, wait times 45–60 min first
   week, soft-opening period preceded official reopening
2. **Gameplay trigger technique resolution**: hold trigger AND time
   aim to the blaster's audio cue on each registered hit; two-handed
   stabilization helps; the tunnel scene is the high-scoring zone
3. Cinderella Castle repainting is actively in progress — one side
   of castle repainted (gray, "back to before the 50th"), Fantasyland
   facade side still pink in 50th anniversary color, Disney working
   systematically around the castle
4. **Big Thunder Mountain Railroad firm reopening date: May 3, 2026**
   — announcement captured live during filming. Height requirement
   lowered from 40" to 38". Trains actively testing on 2026-04-08.
5. Back-of-park construction: Rivers of America mostly filled in,
   Piston Peak foundation work visible, cranes active, concrete
   forms emerging that may become the mountain framework
6. Wait time snapshot 2026-04-08: Buzz 45→60 min, Seven Dwarfs Mine
   Train 80 min posted, Haunted Mansion 50 min, Monsters Inc Laugh
   Floor 20 min (but queue stretched unusually long for a show),
   Speedway 30 min, Little Mermaid 35 min, Small World 45 min
7. Travel time from MK parking lot to park entry: **~20 min minimum**
   (first-class best case, no tram wait)
8. Pinocchio Village House chicken Caesar salad — rare healthier MK
   lunch option, TheTimTracker's favorite MK quick service
9. Her Universe Daisy Duck merchandise collection at Star Traders
10. New sweatshirts ($60), kids jacket ($80), Magic Kingdom spirit
    jersey with Orange Bird branding, hoodie with printed hood
    interior
