## What the source contains

1. **Big Thunder Mountain Railroad rope-drop + reopening demand**: Big
   Thunder is about to reopen; expected Tier 1 Multi Pass. Rope-drop path
   to it should be Liberty Square (not Adventureland) from the hub, or
   the Memento Mori back-corner exit from Fantasyland with Early Theme
   Park Entry.

2. **Tomorrowland timing for Buzz Lightyear rope drop**: Skip the hub
   bridge to Tomorrowland (bottleneck) and walk around via Tomorrowland
   Terrace — slightly longer, but an empty path. Buzz is Tier 2 Multi
   Pass.

3. **Buzz Lightyear gameplay has been rebuilt**: Handheld tethered
   blasters, red/green per side, white-ring double-points, rainbow
   frenzies, scoring into millions. Old "memorize a high-value target"
   strategy is dead. First room of the ride is now an aim-calibration
   room. Creator vs. cast-member disagreement on hold-trigger vs.
   rapid-fire gameplay.

4. **Animal Kingdom is a 5-ride park**: Only Avatar Flight of Passage,
   Na'vi River Journey, Kilimanjaro Safaris, Expedition Everest, and
   Kali River Rapids are operating. Treat AK as a half-day starter park
   that unlocks Lightning Lane reservations at a higher-demand second
   park via the 3-hour window mechanic ("lightning lane loophole").

5. **Free water park admission on check-in day** for Disney Resort
   Collection guests (~$70/person value). Not Swan/Dolphin, not Disney
   Springs hotels, not Bonnet Creek. Typhoon Lagoon (Adventureland
   vibe) vs. Blizzard Beach (Fantasyland vibe). Use as a half-day
   arrival fix, not a full day.

6. **Cool Kids Summer 2026** runs May 26 – Sept 8. Returning from 2025
   with expanded character presence, Goofy Core at EPCOT's Communicore
   Hall, moments at water parks and resorts. "Not something to plan
   around; something not to walk past."

7. **Bluey's World opens May 26, 2026** at Conservation Station /
   Rafiki's Planet Watch (Animal Kingdom). Permanent walkthrough with
   Bluey + Bingo character interactions, games, and adjacent Jumping
   Junction Australian wildlife area. Wildlife Express train closed
   since Feb 23, 2026 to prep. Part of broader AK revitalization.
