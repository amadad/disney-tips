# Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance

Hollywood Studios headliner in Galaxy's Edge and one of the park's most
important Lightning Lane decisions.

## Why it matters

Rise is one of Hollywood Studios's biggest draws, but DFBGuide 2026-04
argues that the right question is not just "How do I skip Rise's line?"
It is "What gives me the best overall day in Hollywood Studios?"

## Single Pass vs. Multi Pass

If you can afford only **one** Lightning Lane product in Hollywood
Studios, DFBGuide's recommendation leans toward
[lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md), **not** a
Single Pass for Rise.

Why:

- Hollywood Studios has a lot of e-ticket demand overall
- Multi Pass gives you broader coverage across the park
- if you are staying at a Disney hotel, you can often use
  **Early Theme Park Entry** to attack Rise early instead

## Free alternatives

DFBGuide calls out two lower-cost ways to reduce the pain here:

- **Early Theme Park Entry** — use the 30-minute on-site head start to
  get in line before the park's first major wave arrives
- **Single rider**, when offered — faster, but it can split your group
  and may bypass parts of the pre-show experience

## Planning takeaway

Rise is still absolutely a ride to take seriously. But for many trips,
its paid line-skip is **less important than getting broader Hollywood
Studios coverage right**.
