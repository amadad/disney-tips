# Strategy: Lightning Lane Park-Hop Between EPCOT and Hollywood Studios

How to use [Lightning Lane Multi Pass](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md) on an
EPCOT + Hollywood Studios day.
The core idea: this is the easiest two-park Lightning Lane strategy in
Walt Disney World because the **Skyliner** makes the transfer simple.

## Why this pair works best

DFBGuide 2026-04's practical argument is straightforward:

- both parks support paid Lightning Lane use well
- both parks are connected by the **Skyliner**
- that makes the park hop less fragile than cross-property hops that
  depend on buses or long walks

## The standard move

1. Start your day in **EPCOT**.
2. Use your three pre-selections there.
3. As soon as you tap into your first EPCOT Lightning Lane, begin booking
   **Hollywood Studios** reservations.
4. Keep replacing used EPCOT reservations with Hollywood Studios ones as
   the day goes on.
5. When you are ready, take the **Skyliner** over and arrive holding a
   stacked set of Hollywood Studios Lightning Lanes.

## Reverse version

DFBGuide also notes that the reverse can work:

- start in **Hollywood Studios**
- stack **EPCOT** reservations for later

Which direction is better depends on what your party wants more:

- more aggressive headliner pressure early → often Hollywood Studios
- more flexible opening with later park-hop ease → often EPCOT

## Why this beats many other park hops

The whole strategy depends on reducing transfer friction.
Skyliner makes this pair uniquely good for a Multi Pass park-hop plan
because it is easier to predict than a bus-heavy cross-property move.
