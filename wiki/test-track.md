# Test Track

Front-half EPCOT thrill ride and one of the park's three core
**Tier 1 Lightning Lane Multi Pass** attractions.

## Why Test Track matters

Test Track is one of the attractions that defines whether EPCOT feels
front-loaded or back-loaded for your group. If this is a priority ride,
the **front entrance** is a more natural park start than International
Gateway.

## Lightning Lane and single rider

- **Tier 1 Multi Pass** attraction
- **Single rider available**

DFBGuide calls out Test Track's single-rider queue as one of the free
wait-cutting tools that can make EPCOT easier without paying for every
possible Lightning Lane.

## Practical implication

At EPCOT, the biggest ride split is often:

- **front entrance priorities** — Test Track and
  [Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind](guardians-of-the-galaxy-cosmic-rewind.md)
- **back entrance priorities** — [Remy's Ratatouille Adventure](remys-ratatouille-adventure.md)
  and the France / UK side of World Showcase
