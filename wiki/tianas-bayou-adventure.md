# Tiana's Bayou Adventure

Magic Kingdom Frontierland flume ride. **Opened in 2024** as a
reimagining of the former **Splash Mountain**, themed to
*The Princess and the Frog*. The queue (which is itself praised by
DisneyInDetail 2026-04 — "the queuing area is great") and indoor dark
ride scenes precede the ride's visible outdoor finale, which features
a **big 50-foot plunge** that gets riders wet. Smaller drops are
interleaved throughout.

With [Big Thunder Mountain Railroad](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md)
closed until its 2026 reopening, Tiana's is currently the **only open
headliner in Frontierland**. All Frontierland demand is compressed
onto this one ride, which keeps its waits elevated relative to its
natural level.

## Wait dynamics

Tiana's is still new enough to Magic Kingdom that waits run long
most of the day, especially on **warm days and in summer** when
getting soaked is itself a draw. DFBGuide recommends hitting it
**at rope drop** or using Lightning Lane Multi Pass — standby in the
afternoon is a long commitment.

**The cold / rainy day exception**: per DisneyInDetail 2026-04, on
cool or rainy days when guests are reluctant to get soaked, Tiana's
waits can **drop to 5 minutes or less**. If your trip hits a cool,
wet day, Tiana's becomes a walk-on and you save your Lightning Lane
slot for something else. Counter-intuitive weather strategy: a bad
weather day is a good Tiana's day.

**If you're worried about the big drop**: the drop is visible from
outside the attraction, so you'll have seen it before boarding. Per
DisneyInDetail, the drop itself is quick and the **rest of the
attraction is worth the moment of fear** — indoor dark-ride scenes,
music, and Princess-and-the-Frog theming extend well beyond the
final plunge. Consider a **poncho** if you want the ride without the
soaking.

## Lightning Lane

Tiana's Bayou Adventure is a **Tier 1** Multi Pass attraction at Magic
Kingdom (along with Jungle Cruise, Space Mountain, and Peter Pan's
Flight). You can only pre-select *one* Tier 1 attraction, so Tiana's
competes with the others for that slot.

**DFBGuide's Tier 1 guidance**:

- If your party is into thrills and willing to get wet, pre-select
  Tiana's as your Tier 1 pick.
- If your party wants a more easygoing option or has members who
  don't want to get soaked, pick [Jungle Cruise](jungle-cruise.md)
  instead.
- [Space Mountain](magic-kingdom.md) and
  [Peter Pan's Flight](peter-pans-flight.md) are worth a Tier 1 slot
  less often because both are accessible during Early Theme Park
  Entry — you may be able to ride one or both for free at rope drop.

See [lightning-lane-multi-pass.md](lightning-lane-multi-pass.md).
