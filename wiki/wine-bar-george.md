# Wine Bar George

Disney Springs wine destination led by master sommelier George Miliotes.
One of the strongest adult-oriented dining / drinking plays in Disney Springs
because its identity is unusually clear: wine-first, with a deep list and a
real reason to visit beyond generic cocktails.

## Why it stands out

DFBGuide highlights three things:

- the wine list is enormous (200+ wines)
- the leadership is unusually credible for a Disney-adjacent venue
- the venue works for both regular meals and brunch

If your group actually cares about wine, this is a much stronger pick than a
random upscale restaurant with a decent bar program.

## Budget alternative

The smart cheaper move here is **The Basket**, the downstairs to-go window.
It lets you sample parts of the Wine Bar George experience without needing the
full sit-down commitment.

## Worth it for

- adults-only groups
- wine-first travelers
- guests looking for a polished Disney Springs brunch or dinner without going
  full steakhouse
