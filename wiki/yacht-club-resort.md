# Yacht Club Resort

Deluxe EPCOT-area resort and the more formal sibling to
[beach-club-resort.md](beach-club-resort.md).
They share the same location advantages and the same pool complex, but
Yacht Club skews more nautical, quieter, and slightly stuffier.

## Why it stands out

Yacht Club still gets the major EPCOT-area benefits:

- walk to EPCOT's International Gateway
- reasonable access to Hollywood Studios
- access to **Stormalong Bay**

What changes is the vibe. DFBGuide frames it as:

- more New England nautical than beachy
- less breezy than Beach Club
- more likely to appeal to guests who want calm over whimsy

## 2026 dining caveat

This source includes a real dated planning issue:

- **Yachtsman Steakhouse** closes in **May 2026**
- expected to reopen **sometime in August 2026**
- **Crew's Cup Lounge** was closed at the time of the video but was
  expected to reopen in May

That means some 2026 stays here will have more limited on-site dining
than usual.

DFBGuide 2026-03 also notes that both Yacht Club and
[Beach Club](beach-club-resort.md) are under routine **exterior
maintenance through 2027**, though **Stormalong Bay remains open**.

## Rooms

Yacht Club offers:

- standard rooms
- club level rooms
- suites

DFBGuide explicitly notes there is **no Disney Vacation Club wing** here.

## Best for

- EPCOT / Hollywood Studios trips that want deluxe convenience
- guests who want Stormalong Bay without Beach Club's softer, more
  playful tone
